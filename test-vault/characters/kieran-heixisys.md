# Kieran Heixisys

Junior lecturer at the [[Duskfrost Academy]], responsible for first-year instruction in foundational theory. He is younger than most of his students expect an elf academic to be — only 89 — and compensates with exceptional preparation. His notes on Duskfrost elemental interaction are used by three senior researchers who have never acknowledged this.

Kieran overcompensates. He arrives at lecture halls an hour early, writes notes that are more thorough than the senior faculty's published papers, and holds office hours that nobody attends because first-years are intimidated by elf lecturers regardless of how approachable they actually are. He is aware of this dynamic and frustrated by it, but addressing it directly would require a social confidence he has not yet developed. He is, by elf standards, barely out of adolescence, and it shows in moments of uncertainty that he covers with additional preparation.

The uncredited use of his research notes is not something he has confronted because confrontation requires seniority he does not have. Instead, he has begun embedding minor deliberate errors in the notes he leaves accessible — not enough to corrupt research, but enough to identify which conclusions in senior publications originated from his work. He considers this evidence gathering rather than sabotage. [[Professor Taevis Wrent]] is the most frequent beneficiary of his uncredited work, and the one Kieran resents least, because Wrent at least improves upon what he borrows.

He has a cautious admiration for [[Registrar Morna Pelwick]], who is the only person at the Academy who treats him with the same professional courtesy she extends to tenured faculty. He suspects this is because she judges people by their paperwork rather than their age, and his paperwork is impeccable.
