# Scrivener Tulla Nance

Public scrivener in [[Bellsummit]]'s civic quarter — she writes letters, petitions, contracts, and the occasional document that occupies a creative relationship with official certification. She does not forge; she assists clients in producing documents that are accurate to their needs and ambiguous on details that would complicate official review. This distinction is legally defensible and has been tested twice. She won both times.

Maintains a professional relationship with [[Mivi]].
