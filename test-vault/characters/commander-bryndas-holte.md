# Commander Bryndas Holte

[[Solarguard]] commander at [[Scorchhelm]], responsible for regional military operations across the eastern Duskfrost approaches. He is a career officer who rose through the ranks on demonstrated competence rather than political connection — a rarity that has made him both respected and occasionally inconvenient to his superiors. He maintains the [[Compact Vault]] access protocols personally and has declined three requests from [[Gorduin Wynlar]] to share copies of the documents therein.

Holte's office at Scorchhelm is sparse — a desk, maps, a single chair for visitors that is deliberately uncomfortable. He believes comfort breeds complacency and applies this philosophy to himself first. He wakes before dawn, inspects the perimeter rotation personally twice a week, and eats the same rations as his lowest-ranked soldiers. This is not performance. He genuinely cannot taste the difference between officer's fare and standard issue, and sees no reason to pretend otherwise.

The refusal to share Compact Vault documents with Gorduin is not obstinacy — it is fear. Holte has read those documents. Some of them describe contingency protocols for situations that are beginning to look uncomfortably plausible, and he has decided that limiting access is more responsible than enabling academic curiosity, however well-intentioned. He has not told Gorduin why, because explaining would require sharing the very information he is trying to contain.

[[Lieutenant Orvaine Shel]] is the only officer he trusts with the intelligence operation's full scope. He promoted her over three more senior candidates and has never explained the decision publicly. Privately, he considers her the most competent officer in his command and wishes she did not have to work twice as hard to prove it.
