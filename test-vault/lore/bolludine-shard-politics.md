# Bolludine Shard Politics

The Bolludine family controls the only working set of long-distance communication shards in Duskfrost — seventeen paired devices that allow near-instant message relay across the city and to three external locations. The family's pricing, access policies, and occasional refusal of service have become a recurring political crisis. Three Triumvirate attempts to nationalize the shards have failed on the same legal technicality, which most legal scholars believe the Bolludines wrote into the relevant statute themselves.
