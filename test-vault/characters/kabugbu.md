# Kabugbu

An orc who operates as an intelligence asset within the [[Bonehands]] organization — someone who provides information to outside parties at significant personal risk. The 10% trigger probability and 5-generation cooldown reflect his real-world status: he is hard to reach, rarely available, and cannot be contacted consecutively without compromise risk.

Maintains a professional relationship with [[Gorduin Wynlar]].
