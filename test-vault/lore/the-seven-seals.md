# The Seven Seals

The Seven Seals — referred to in some Pallasys translations as the Seventeen Seals, a discrepancy that has not been resolved — are pre-Calamity arcane constructs placed at what appear to be key nodes in the resonance network. Their purpose is not fully understood; the translations describe them variously as "locks," "governors," and "the memory of the agreement." At least two Seals are believed to be located within or near Duskfrost. Neither has been found. The [[Oracles of Might]] consider this a priority research area and do not discuss it publicly.
