# Jafrom Deepbeard

Dwarf master stonemason who has held the maintenance contract for the [[Bellsummit]] tower complex for twenty-two years. He knows which walls are load-bearing, which are decorative, and which ones the Oracles have asked him not to look at directly. He honors all three categories with equal professionalism. His beard reaches his belt and is legendary in the local craftworkers' lodge.

Jafrom can read a wall the way a physician reads a pulse. He places his hand flat against the surface, closes his eyes, and tells you within a decade when the mortar was laid and within a century who cut the stone. This is not magic — it is 178 years of paying attention to the only material that matters. He considers wood a temporary affectation and metal a necessary compromise. Stone is forever, and his job is to make sure it stays that way.

The walls he has been told not to look at bother him on a professional level. He can feel the structural stress through the masonry — something behind those walls is exerting pressure that was not part of the original architectural design, and it has been increasing over the past five years. He has submitted three maintenance reports flagging the issue in technical language that the [[Oracles of Might]] acknowledged and did not act upon. He has not pressed the matter further, but he has reinforced the adjacent load-bearing walls on his own initiative, because he will not have a collapse on his record.

He and [[Grolgrurim Bluntgut]] share a craftworkers' lodge membership and a mutual respect grounded in the dwarf understanding that a man is defined by the quality of his work and the length of his grudges. Jafrom's grudges are short. His work is excellent.
