# Canopic Chest of Serendipity

A small stone chest of Antecedent Culture manufacture that produces a random minor beneficial effect each time it is opened — a coin appears, a mild wound closes, a misplaced object is found nearby. The effects are thoroughly mundane. The mechanism is completely incomprehensible. The Academy has studied it for forty years.

Currently stored in the [[Oracles of Might]] restricted collection.
