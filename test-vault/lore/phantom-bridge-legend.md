# Phantom Bridge Legend

[[Highwallow]] residents have reported a recurring visual phenomenon near the Scar's eastern boundary: the apparent outline of a bridge that does not exist, visible at dawn and dusk in certain atmospheric conditions. The Academy's official position is optical refraction caused by the Scar's arcane turbulence. The unofficial position of most Highwallow residents is that the bridge is a memory of something that stood there before the Calamity.

Understanding requires familiarity with the [[Guild Hierarchy Laws]].
