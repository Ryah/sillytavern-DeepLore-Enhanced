# Resurrection Jar

A ceramic jar of ancient make that allegedly holds the preserved essence of a soul after death, preventing final departure for a limited window. The window is believed to be 72 hours. No successful demonstration has been recorded. The jar is currently in private collection in [[Bellsummit]].

Like the [[Divine Tiara]], its provenance suggests Antecedent Culture manufacture, though the craftsmanship is notably different.
