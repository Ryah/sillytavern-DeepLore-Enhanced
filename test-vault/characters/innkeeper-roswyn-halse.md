# Innkeeper Roswyn Halse

Owner and innkeeper of [[The Hidden Dragonfruit Tavern]] in [[Bayside Shushail]]'s harbor district. The Dragonfruit operates under a strictly maintained neutrality policy: no violence, no business discussed at the bar, no enforcement of outside quarrels inside the establishment. Roswyn enforces this policy personally and has ejected people significantly more dangerous than her frame suggests. She knows who her regulars are and what they do, and considers both categories of information strictly confidential.
