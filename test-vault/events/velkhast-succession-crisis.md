# Velkhast Succession Crisis

The death of [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] head Roderic Velkhast without a designated heir has created a succession dispute now in its second year. Three claimants are actively competing. The [[Solarguard]] has aligned with the second, [[Corwin Velkhast]]. The [[Bonehands]] back the third, a distant cousin with harbor connections. The first claimant disappeared eight months ago under circumstances that remain unresolved — no body was recovered, and the Triumvirate council has refused to declare a formal death, which keeps Corwin from confirmation. Persistent rumors in [[Highwallow]] claim the first claimant is alive and living under an assumed name in the marsh district, protected by contacts who want the succession frozen indefinitely.

The crisis has echoes of the [[Night of Empty Chairs]] (Year 178), a historical precedent that ended an earlier Velkhast succession dispute through assassination rather than negotiation.
