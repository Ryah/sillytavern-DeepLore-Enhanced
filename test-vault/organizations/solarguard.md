# Solarguard

The Solarguard is Duskfrost's official law enforcement body, established in Year 243 under a charter negotiated between all three Triumvirate houses and the Academy. Approximately 400 officers patrol the city across three shifts.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: Enforcement | Founded: Year 243 | HQ: [[Scorchhelm]] | Commander: Warden-Captain Orin Castellan | Officers: ~400 | Jurisdiction: All districts (nominal)]
</div>

The Solarguard wears grey and gold — silver armor with gold insignia for officers. Their authority is theoretically city-wide, but practical enforcement varies dramatically by district. In [[Bellsummit]] and [[Amberburgh]], they are visible, effective, and relatively uncorrupt. In [[Bayside Shushail]] and [[Highwallow]], their patrols are rare, their investigations rarely completed, and their relationship with the [[Bonehands]] is an open secret that no one in command is willing to officially acknowledge.

**Commander Orin Castellan** is a 50-year-old half-elf who rose through the ranks over 28 years of service. She is broadly respected for competence and narrow personal integrity, and broadly criticized for institutional tolerance of corruption below her. Her alignment in the Velkhast succession dispute is with the second claimant, a fact she would deny if asked directly.

**Relationship with the Academy:** The Solarguard's [[Wardenship]] liaison program gives the Academy enforcement authority over registered practitioners. In practice, this means the Solarguard handles street crime and the Wardenship handles anything arcane, and each pretends the other's jurisdiction is not ambiguous.

**Key Officers:** [[Commander Bryndas Holte]] serves as Castellan's second-in-command and handles day-to-day operational assignments. [[Lieutenant Orvaine Shel]] runs the Bellsummit precinct and is considered Castellan's most reliable district commander. [[Corporal Thresh Dunmore]] leads the harbor patrol unit — a thankless posting in Bonehands territory that Dunmore handles with stubborn professionalism. [[Kasper Lampi]], a civilian investigator attached to the Solarguard under the Academy liaison program, provides arcane forensics support and has been involved in every major magical crime investigation of the last five years.

**Relationship with the Bonehands:** Officially hostile. Practically: a standing cold war with occasional hot incidents in Bayside Shushail. Several Solarguard officers in the harbor district are on Bonehands payroll, a fact that Castellan cannot prove and cannot entirely ignore.
