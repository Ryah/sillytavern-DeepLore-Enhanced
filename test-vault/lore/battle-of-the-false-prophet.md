# Battle of the False Prophet

The Battle of the False Prophet (Year 201) occupies a strange place in Duskfrost history — significant enough to have changed the city's trajectory, obscure enough that most residents have never heard of it.

A charismatic figure appeared in the [[Highwallow]] district in Year 198, claiming to be a prophet of the pre-Calamity civilization with knowledge of how to prevent its recurrence. They attracted a following of approximately three thousand people over two years — a meaningful fraction of the city's then-population.

The prophet's teachings were, on examination, a carefully constructed synthesis of genuine pre-Calamity scholarship (stolen from the Archive) and fabricated revelation designed to concentrate power in the prophet's hands. The fabrication was discovered by an Archive researcher in Year 200. The discovery was suppressed — the Triumvirate feared the revelation would cause riots.

When the prophet's followers discovered the suppression in Year 201, the riots happened anyway, escalating quickly into organized violence. The "battle" was actually three days of urban fighting between prophet loyalists and Triumvirate forces, concentrated in Highwallow and [[Amberburgh]]. Forty-seven people died. The prophet disappeared on the first day and was never found.

The event led directly to the [[Wardenship]]'s expansion and the establishment of the Archive's counter-disinformation research program, both of which are still active.
