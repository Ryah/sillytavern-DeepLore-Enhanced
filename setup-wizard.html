Welcome to DeepLore Enhanced

Your Obsidian vault, wired into SillyTavern.

What is this?

DeepLore Enhanced connects your Obsidian lore vault to SillyTavern, automatically injecting relevant worldbuilding entries into your roleplay context. Your characters, locations, lore, and rules — pulled in when they matter.

How does it work?

  1. Keywords in chat text match against your vault entries
  2. AI refinement (optional) picks the most relevant matches
  3. Entries are injected into the AI's context window automatically

This wizard will walk you through the full setup. You can change everything later in Settings.
