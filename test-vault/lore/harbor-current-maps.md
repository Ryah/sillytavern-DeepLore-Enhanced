# Harbor Current Maps

The [[Bayside Shushail]] current maps are maintained by the Harbor Authority and updated after every significant storm event. The harbor has three primary approach channels; the northern channel is impassable from late autumn through early spring due to current inversion caused by the underwater thermal vents first mapped in Year 244. All vessels entering at night are required to carry a licensed harbor pilot.
