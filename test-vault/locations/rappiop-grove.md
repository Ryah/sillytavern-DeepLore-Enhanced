# Rappiop Grove

A park and garden district in [[Amberburgh]] maintained under Academy contract. Known for its living hedges ([[Thyren]]-grown), outdoor study pavilions, and the fact that nearly everyone who meets clandestinely in Duskfrost eventually ends up in Rappiop Grove. The resonance grid fragment was discovered beneath the eastern garden.

Trade routes connect this area to [[Bonehands Counting House]].
