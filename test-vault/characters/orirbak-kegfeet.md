# Orirbak Kegfeet

Dwarf master brewer and serving treasurer for the [[Marblesmiths]] guild in [[Amberburgh]]. The brewing and the treasury are not unrelated — he finances the guild's social functions through his brewery's surplus, which gives him a structural relationship with everyone who attends guild events. He has never used this leverage overtly. He does not need to.
