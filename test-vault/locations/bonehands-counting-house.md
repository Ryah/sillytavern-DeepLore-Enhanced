# Bonehands Counting House

A legitimately licensed financial services office on the primary dock street of [[Bayside Shushail]] that processes currency exchange, shipping insurance, and debt collection for the harbor district. It also happens to be the Bonehands' administrative headquarters, which everyone knows and no one says aloud within earshot of the door.

Shares administrative ties with [[Rimeborough]].
