# Champions Chapel

A small, austere chapel within the [[Champions of Patience]] barracks complex in [[Scorchhelm]]. Used for investiture ceremonies, renewal of oaths, and the daily contemplative practice the order requires of active members. Non-members are permitted entry only by invitation from a Knight Exemplar or above.

Shares administrative ties with [[Bellsummit]].
