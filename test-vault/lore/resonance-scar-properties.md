# Resonance Scar Properties

The Resonance Scar is a persistent zone of arcane turbulence in [[Highwallow]], a direct consequence of the Calamity's grid collapse at the [[Ley Line Geography of Duskfrost|ley line convergence point]]. Sigils drawn in the Scar zone have a measurable (approximately 12%) chance of behaving unexpectedly. The boundary fluctuates seasonally. The [[Duskfrost Academy]] has been mapping it since Year 198 with incomplete results.

The Scar occupies roughly eighteen city blocks at its minimum extent and twenty-six at maximum, expanding during winter and contracting during summer in a cycle that the Academy has measured consistently for over a century. Within the Scar boundary, arcane energy behaves as [[Resonance Frequency Theory|broadband noise]] — all frequency bands active simultaneously, drowning out the specific resonance patterns that sigils require for reliable function. The 12% malfunction rate is an average; near the Scar's center, the rate approaches 40%, and at the absolute core — a zone approximately fifty meters in diameter that no one enters voluntarily — sigils do not malfunction so much as improvise. A heating sigil might produce light. A warding sigil might produce sound. A diagnostic sigil, in one documented case, produced a smell that the Academy's report describes as "existential dread, olfactory."

The Scar's boundary is not a sharp line but a gradient. The outermost zone, where most [[Scar Dweller Testimonies|Scar-adjacent residents]] live, shows subtle effects: tools with arcane components occasionally fail, dreams are unusually vivid, and animals refuse to cross certain thresholds. The middle zone is where the 12% malfunction rate applies and where most of the [[Highwallow Underground Economy|unlicensed practitioners]] operate — the interference is strong enough to mask their work from Academy detection but not strong enough to make the work impossible. The inner zone is a no-go area by Academy directive, though enforcement is inconsistent and the desperate, the curious, and the foolish enter regularly. Most return. The ones who do not are assumed to have encountered the Scar's most dangerous property: spatial disorientation. Deep within the Scar, distances and directions become unreliable. A person walking in a straight line may find themselves somewhere they did not intend, including underground in the [[Resonance Scar Tunnels|Scar tunnel network]].

The Academy's mapping project has produced over a century of boundary data that reveals a deeply uncomfortable trend. The Scar is shrinking. Not quickly — approximately 0.3% per year on average — but consistently, over a timeframe that rules out normal fluctuation. The [[Solstice Alignment Ritual|Oracles' solstice measurements]] confirm the trend. A shrinking Scar sounds like good news. It is not. A shrinking Scar means the energy is going somewhere — concentrating rather than dissipating. The Academy's models suggest the energy is being pulled toward the Scar's center, where the original resonance tower node stood. What happens when four centuries of accumulated arcane energy finishes concentrating at a single point is a question the Academy is working on with visible urgency and no public timeline.
