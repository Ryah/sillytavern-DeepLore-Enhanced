# Scourge of Vitality

The Scourge of Vitality is a small cult operating covertly in [[Highwallow]], extracting life-force from marginal populations to power extended-duration binding rituals. The [[Solarguard]] has tried to dismantle them twice. Both times, key members escaped prosecution.
