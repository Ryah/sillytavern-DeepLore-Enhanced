# War of Kroyhr

A pre-Calamity conflict between two Antecedent Culture city-states that apparently involved the weaponization of resonance grid energy. Fragmentary records suggest it ended when both sides destroyed each other's towers simultaneously.

Scholars studying the conflict have drawn parallels to the political dynamics behind the [[Watchers Treaty]].
