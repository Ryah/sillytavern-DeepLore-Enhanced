# Dorian Schmitt

Triumvirate herald and public affairs officer responsible for official announcements, ceremonial proclamations, and the management of what the public is told and when. He has a voice trained for projection and a face trained for inscrutability. He knows more state secrets than most administrators two levels above him, purely because he has to know what he is not announcing.

Dorian was trained as a performer before he entered government service — three years at a Stormhaven theatrical academy before the money ran out. He considers public speaking a form of performance where the script is written by committee and the audience's reaction has political consequences. He drafts his own proclamations when allowed and edits them when not, because whoever writes the words controls the emphasis, and emphasis is everything.

He reports directly to the [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] leadership, which means he receives information before it is approved for release and must decide, in the gap between receiving and announcing, how to frame it. This gap is where his real power lies. He cannot change the facts, but he can choose which facts lead and which follow, and the ordering of facts is a form of argument that most listeners do not consciously recognize. He is very good at this, and it troubles him more than it used to.

He has an adversarial respect for [[Herald Daveth Ruun]], who controls access to the First Speaker while Dorian controls the Speaker's public voice. They are, functionally, two halves of the same gatekeeping function, and each considers the other slightly more dangerous than the Triumvirate appreciates.
