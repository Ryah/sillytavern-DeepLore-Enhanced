# Herald Daveth Ruun

Personal aide and herald to the First Speaker of the Triumvirate — the person who controls access to the highest level of Triumvirate executive authority. He is the last gatekeeper before the First Speaker's schedule, the person who decides which communications are urgent enough to interrupt and which can wait. He has held this position for eight years across two First Speakers, which means his institutional knowledge substantially exceeds that of his current principal.

Daveth dresses in the exact regulation attire for his station — never more, never less. He considers personal style a form of opinion, and opinions are things he cannot afford to display. He keeps the First Speaker's schedule in his memory rather than on paper, because paper can be stolen and memory cannot be subpoenaed. He sleeps four hours a night and considers this sufficient.

His position within the [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] makes him one of the most politically sensitive appointments in the city. What nobody in the compound discusses openly is that Daveth survived the transition between First Speakers — an event that typically results in a complete staff replacement. He survived because both the outgoing and incoming Speaker recognized that replacing him would mean losing eight years of procedural knowledge that exists nowhere else. He is aware that this makes him indispensable, and that indispensable people in political positions eventually become threats. He has a contingency for this, which he has shared with no one.

He maintains a carefully neutral relationship with [[Steward Pela Forthwick]], whom he encounters daily in the compound. They exchange exactly the information required by their respective roles and nothing more. Each suspects the other knows more than they share, and both are correct.
