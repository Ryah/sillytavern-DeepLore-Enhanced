# Scorchhelm

Scorchhelm is built on and around the ruins of the pre-Calamity fortification — the oldest intentionally military structure in the area. Three centuries of Reconstruction construction have buried most of the original beneath what is now the [[Solarguard Headquarters]] complex, barracks, training grounds, and armories.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: Fortress District | Elevation: Mid-cliff, eastern edge | Watch Presence: Maximum | Social Class: Military/working | Key Institutions: [[Solarguard]] HQ, [[Champions of Patience]] barracks, [[Compact Vault]] (sub-level) | Population: ~4,000 + stationed forces]
</div>

The district's character is functional and institutional. Streets are grid-planned and wide enough for formation movement. Buildings are stone, low-ornamentation, and built to last rather than impress. The Solarguard's grey-and-gold banners fly from twelve points throughout.

The Compact Vault is three levels below the fortress — accessible only with the three Triumvirate keys plus Academy authorization. The Solarguard guards the entrance but is not authorized to enter.

The Champions of Patience maintain a barracks on the district's southern edge, separate from Solarguard facilities by old institutional habit and current mutual preference.

Three sections of the original pre-Calamity fortification are accessible for research under Academy supervision: the sigil chambers (partially active), the command level (structurally sound, mostly empty), and the deep tunnels (partially mapped, connection to [[The Hollow Roads]] suspected).

Located within reach of [[Bellsummit]].
