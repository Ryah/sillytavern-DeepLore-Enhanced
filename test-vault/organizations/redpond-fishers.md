# Redpond Fishers Cooperative

The Redpond Fishers Cooperative holds the city license for freshwater fishing in the Redpond lake district. Small operation (18 members), but they supply a meaningful fraction of the city's freshwater fish. The [[Harcyne Granary Authority]] has been trying to absorb them for twenty years.
