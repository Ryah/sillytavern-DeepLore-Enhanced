# Lorant

The Lorant are a winged humanoid species who nest on the cliff faces above [[Bellsummit]], in a vertical settlement they call the Roostwall — a network of carved ledges, wind-shelters, and thermal vents accessible only by flight. Approximately 180 Lorant live in Duskfrost, all on the upper cliffs. They do not live at street level. They do not want to.

Lorant wings are fully functional for sustained flight, not merely gliding. An adult Lorant can stay airborne for six hours in favorable thermals and carry up to half their body weight while doing so. This makes them the fastest point-to-point couriers in the city, a monopoly they have defended with legal precision and occasional physical intimidation. The Aerial Messenger Treaty — signed with the [[Triumvirate]] eighty years ago and renewed every decade — grants the Lorant exclusive rights to licensed aerial courier service in exchange for a modest tax and a commitment to serve all districts impartially. The [[Bolludine]] have challenged this monopoly repeatedly. The Lorant have won every challenge, in part because their legal arguments are sound and in part because the treaty predates the Bolludine courier networks by forty years.

Lorant culture is intensely vertical. Status is literal — higher nests indicate older, more established families. The highest ledges on the Roostwall belong to the Windelders, a council of senior Lorant who govern the community's internal affairs with minimal reference to surface politics. Decisions are made in open-air assemblies held at altitude, which has the practical effect of excluding non-Lorant from participation. This is not accidental.

They are territorial in ways that surface species sometimes misread as aggression. A Lorant does not share airspace casually. Flying too close to the Roostwall without invitation is treated as trespass, and the Lorant enforce their boundaries with warning dives — a controlled swoop that passes close enough to displace the air around the intruder's head. The [[Solarguard]] has learned not to send patrol griffons near the cliffs without prior arrangement.

Relations with the surface city are transactional and correct. Lorant deliver messages, carry light cargo, and provide aerial survey data to the [[Wardenship]] during construction projects. They purchase food, tools, and manufactured goods from [[Bellsummit]] markets via designated landing platforms at the [[Cliff Stairs]]. They attend Triumvirate functions when protocol requires and leave promptly when it does not. Their ambassador to the [[Bellsummit Council]] — a rotating position held for one year — is understood to speak for the Windelders, not for individual Lorant, and the distinction matters.

Among other species, the Lorant have the closest relationship with the [[Delivese]], whose Rimeborough settlement sits near the northern cliff face. The two communities share a preference for altitude, cold wind, and being left alone — a foundation sturdy enough for mutual respect, if not warmth.
