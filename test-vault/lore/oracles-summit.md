# Oracles Summit Protocol

The Summit Protocol is the [[Oracles of Might]]'s internal procedure for emergency disclosure of classified research to the full senior membership. It can only be invoked by two senior members acting jointly — preventing unilateral disclosure while ensuring nothing critical dies with a single researcher.

The Protocol requires:
1. Both invoking members physically present in the Summit Chamber (third sub-level, [[Bellsummit]] tower)
2. A written statement of the emergency disclosure rationale, signed and witnessed
3. All senior members present or represented by sealed proxy
4. The Grand Oracle (or designated substitute) to formally receive the disclosure

The Protocol was last invoked six years ago when the [[Angel Crown]] began changing its inscription. The disclosure resulted in a six-month research suspension while the tower was re-warded.

[[Gorduin Wynlar]] and [[Ayre Waesphyra]] are the most likely pair to invoke the Protocol at any given time — they are the two researchers with the broadest access to classified materials and the longest track record of disagreeing with management about what constitutes an emergency.
