# Professor Taevis Wrent

Professor of theoretical magic at the [[Bellsummit]] Academy of Arcane Arts, specializing in the interaction between Duskfrost's ambient magical field and conventional arcane practice. He has published fourteen monographs and is working on a fifteenth that will, he hopes, resolve an argument he has been having in the literature with a Stormhaven researcher for six years. He is popular with advanced students and somewhat frightening to first-years.

Maintains a professional relationship with [[Kieran Heixisys]].
