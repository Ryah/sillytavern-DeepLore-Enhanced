# Resonance Grid Discovery

A junior Academy researcher discovered a functioning fragment of the pre-Calamity resonance grid beneath [[Rappiop Grove]] approximately two years ago. The Academy has suppressed public knowledge of this discovery. All three Triumvirate houses are aware. None have publicly acknowledged it.

This event is directly connected to the [[War of Glimmering Hope]].
