# Vulfun Gurdoth

Dark elf fence who operates a respectable-looking import stall in [[Bayside Shushail]]'s night markets — the respectability is genuine, and so is the secondary business in goods that have changed hands without full paperwork. He is well-mannered, patient, and maintains excellent records of everything except the things that would implicate him.

Vulfun's stall displays imported ceramics, small textiles, and decorative metalwork — all legitimate, all tastefully arranged, all priced to move. The legitimate business is profitable enough to justify the stall. The secondary business is profitable enough to justify everything else. He receives goods through channels that the [[Bonehands]] tolerate because he pays his percentage promptly, and he sells them through channels that the [[Solarguard]] overlooks because his operation is small, clean, and does not generate violence.

What makes Vulfun unusual among Bayside Shushail's criminal economy is his patience. He does not push for volume, does not handle anything that would attract serious investigation, and turns away goods that are too distinctive to resell safely. He has been fencing for thirty years and has never been charged with anything, which he attributes to three principles: never be greedy, never be memorable, and never handle anything someone powerful will miss.

His one vulnerability is a genuine friendship with [[Zuni Dhegevnac]], which predates both their professional careers by decades. They grew up in the same dark elf community outside Duskfrost, and Vulfun is one of the very few people who knew Zuni before she became what she is now. He does not trade on this connection. He is, however, the person she sends messages through when she does not trust her usual channels, and this makes him more important than his modest operation suggests.
