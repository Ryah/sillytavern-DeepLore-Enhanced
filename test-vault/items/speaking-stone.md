# Speaking Stone

Paired stones that allow two-way communication between holders regardless of distance, within the same city. Pre-Calamity manufacture. The Academy produces weak imitations at significant expense. True Speaking Stones are rare and enormously valuable. Three verified pairs are known to exist in Duskfrost.

The communication principle appears related to the binding resonance used in the [[Mirror of Binding]].
