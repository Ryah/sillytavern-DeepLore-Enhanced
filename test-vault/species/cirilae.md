# Cirilae

The Cirilae are a species of humanoids with dense, stone-like skin that varies from granite grey to sandstone ochre. They are slow but extraordinarily durable — a working Cirilae can maintain sustained physical labor for eighteen hours without meaningful fatigue. This quality made them the primary construction workforce during Duskfrost's reconstruction period, a history that defines their social position in the city today.

Cirilae have minimal arcane affinity, which in pre-Calamity society would have placed them near the bottom of the social hierarchy. In post-Calamity Duskfrost, their immunity to arcane turbulence in the Resonance Scar zone made them uniquely valuable for construction work in [[Highwallow]], where other species experienced disorientation and health effects.

That historical service bought the Cirilae community political goodwill that has not entirely worn away. They hold two seats on the [[Amberburgh]] district council and maintain a cultural hall in the [[Saseopt District]]. The [[Marblesmiths]] guild is majority-Cirilae.

Cirilae culture places high value on patience and completion. Beginning something and not finishing it is considered a moral failing. This extends to relationships, arguments, and debts — a Cirilae who owes a favor will remember it for decades and consider it unfinished business.

They share workspace with [[Crennish]] cataloguers in the Archive, where their complementary skills — durability and memory — make them effective partners.
