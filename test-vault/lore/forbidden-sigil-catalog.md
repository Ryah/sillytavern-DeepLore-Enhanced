# Forbidden Sigil Catalog

The Forbidden Sigil Catalog is a partial reconstruction of the Antecedent Authority's restricted sigil register — a list of arcane patterns deemed too dangerous for general use. Of the estimated 340 entries, [[Thornwick Pallasys]] translated approximately 80. The [[Duskfrost Academy]] keeps these under Tier 3 restriction. Twelve of the translated sigils are known to still function correctly.

The catalog's organization follows the Antecedent Authority's own classification system, which Pallasys partially decoded: sigils are grouped by effect category (destructive, binding, perceptual, dimensional, and a fifth category whose name translates approximately as "self-modifying"), then ranked by a danger scale that appears to run from one to nine. The highest-ranked sigils in the translated portion are all in the "self-modifying" category — patterns that, once drawn, alter themselves. The Academy's theoretical department considers self-modifying sigils the most dangerous class of arcane construct because they violate the fundamental assumption of [[Sigil Theory — Fundamentals|sigil theory]]: that the geometry determines the effect. A sigil that changes its own geometry can change its own effect, recursively, with no predictable endpoint.

Of the twelve functional sigils, the Academy has tested four under controlled conditions. Two produced effects consistent with Pallasys's translations — a localized gravity inversion and a short-duration temporal perception shift. One produced an effect entirely different from the translation (Pallasys called it "voice of stillness"; it actually induces uncontrollable weeping in everyone within earshot). The fourth test was discontinued after the containment wards began resonating sympathetically with the sigil, suggesting the sigil was attempting to recruit the wards into its own pattern. The remaining eight functional sigils have not been tested. The Academy's position is that testing will resume when containment protocols are improved. The containment protocols have not been improved in fourteen years.

[[Gorduin Wynlar]] keeps the only complete copy of Pallasys's translations under personal guard in the [[Oracles of Might]] vault. The Academy's official copy is redacted — twenty-three sigils removed at Gorduin's insistence, on the grounds that their effects, if replicated, would constitute an existential threat to Duskfrost. The Academy's Restriction Committee agreed unanimously, which is notable because the Committee agrees unanimously on almost nothing. The redacted sigils are referred to internally as "the Wynlar Exclusions," and their contents are known to exactly three living people. Gorduin has stated that he will destroy his copy before allowing it to be captured. Whether this is a bluff is a question no one has been willing to test.
