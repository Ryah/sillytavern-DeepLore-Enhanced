# Severance Dagger

A dagger with a blade that appears to cut arcane connections as readily as physical ones. A single cut from it will sever a binding contract, break an active ward, or disrupt an enchantment — permanently, with no restoration possible. The [[Wardenship]] classified it as an Extreme Hazard artifact and lost track of it in Year 318.
