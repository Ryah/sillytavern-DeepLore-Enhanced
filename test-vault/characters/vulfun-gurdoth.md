# Vulfun Gurdoth

Dark elf fence who operates a respectable-looking import stall in [[Bayside Shushail]]'s night markets — the respectability is genuine, and so is the secondary business in goods that have changed hands without full paperwork. He is well-mannered, patient, and maintains excellent records of everything except the things that would implicate him. His scene_type:social flag reflects that he is only useful — and only reachable — in social contexts.
