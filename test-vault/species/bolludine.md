# Bolludine

Bolludines are small (averaging 4'2") insectoid humanoids with iridescent chitinous shells that shift color with the ambient light. They are extraordinarily fast — physically and conversationally — and have built a reputation as the most reliable couriers and information brokers in Duskfrost.

[Type: Species | Origin: Forest | Lifespan: ~80 years | Height: 3'10"-4'6" | Shell: Iridescent chitin | Eyes: Compound, gold | Speed: Exceptional | Social: Highly networked]
</div>

Bolludine possess compound eyes with a wider field of vision than any other Duskfrost species. They sleep in short 2-3 hour bursts up to four times per day rather than a single long sleep, which means their information networks operate effectively around the clock.

Their community in Duskfrost centers on the [[Amberburgh]] market district, where three extended family networks (called shards) dominate the courier, message, and small-goods trade. The shards compete fiercely with each other but present a unified front to non-Bolludine business interests.

Bolludine are frequently underestimated due to their size and the flippant style of their communication. This is, as many have learned too late, a significant mistake. They keep meticulous records of every business transaction and social slight, and they are patient in ways their rapid speech patterns do not suggest.
