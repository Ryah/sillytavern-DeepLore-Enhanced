# Duskfrost Geography

Duskfrost occupies a coastal bluff with its wealthiest districts clustered at the top and its poorest spilling down toward the harbor and marshes.

**[[Bellsummit]]** — The high district. Academies, libraries, noble estates, and the Triumvirate council hall. Clean streets, heavy watch presence.

**[[Amberburgh]]** — The merchant district. Guilds, markets, warehouses, and counting houses. Middle-class, busy, politically neutral.

**[[Bayside Shushail]]** — The harbor district. Docks, fisheries, sailors' taverns, smuggling rings. Ostensibly watched by the [[Solarguard]] but in practice controlled by the [[Bonehands]].

**[[Scorchhelm]]** — The old fortress district. Barracks, armories, the city watch headquarters. Built on the ruins of the original pre-Calamity fortification.

**[[Highwallow]]** — The marsh district. Flood-prone, overcrowded, neglected. Home to the city's poorest residents, several unlicensed magic practitioners, and rumored connections to [[The Hollow Roads]] below.

**East Hamp / South Sool / [[Saseopt District]]** — Residential middle districts. Quiet, unremarkable, densely populated.

**[[Rappiop Grove]]** — A park and garden district maintained by the Academy. Popular for outdoor study and clandestine meetings.
