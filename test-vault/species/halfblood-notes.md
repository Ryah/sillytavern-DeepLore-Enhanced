# Halfbloods and Mixed Heritage

Mixed-heritage individuals — typically called halfbloods informally and persons of mixed lineage formally — exist across all species combinations in Duskfrost. They often inherit partial traits from both parent species and may or may not have arcane affinity depending on the combination.

Social acceptance varies by district. [[Bellsummit]] is formally inclusive. [[Highwallow]] is practically inclusive out of necessity. [[Scorchhelm]] has a notable prejudice problem the [[Solarguard]] has repeatedly failed to address.
