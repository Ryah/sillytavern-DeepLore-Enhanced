# Academy Alumni Fellowship

The Academy Alumni Fellowship is the social and professional network for graduates of the [[Duskfrost Academy]]. It holds quarterly dinners, maintains an employment referral network, and lobbies the Academy administration on matters of alumni interest. Its political influence exceeds its formal importance.

Several prominent alumni maintain dual membership in the [[Salvagers Compact]], which the Fellowship officially neither endorses nor discourages.
