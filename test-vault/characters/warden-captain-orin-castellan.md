# Warden-Captain Orin Castellan

Orin Castellan has commanded the [[Solarguard]] for eleven years, which is long enough to have outlasted two Triumvirate power shifts, one attempted coup, and the ongoing expectation that she will eventually do something about the [[Bonehands]]. She has not. Whether this reflects strategic patience or institutional capture depends on who you ask and how much they owe her.

Castellan rose through the Solarguard ranks over twenty-eight years of service, starting as a harbor patrol officer in [[Bayside Shushail]] — a posting that taught her exactly how the city's real economy functions. She does not discuss those years in detail. The lessons, however, inform every decision she makes as commander.

Her personal integrity is narrow and genuine: she does not take bribes, does not accept favors from the Triumvirate houses, and does not look the other way when officers under her direct supervision break the rules. The problem — which she acknowledges privately and never publicly — is that her direct supervision covers perhaps forty of her four hundred officers. The rest operate in a grey zone she has neither the resources nor the political capital to clean up.

Her position on the [[Velkhast Succession Crisis]] is with the second claimant, a fact she would deny under oath. She considers the first claimant likely dead and the third claimant a [[Bonehands]] proxy. This assessment is shared by [[Commander Bryndas Holte]], who commands the regional military side of things and with whom Castellan maintains a productive, mutually respectful, and deliberately arm's-length working relationship.

Her half-elven heritage makes her a political oddity in [[Bellsummit]] circles where pure lineage still matters to people with nothing more important to worry about. She has never used it as leverage and never acknowledged it as a liability, which is itself a form of leverage.
