# Keepers of the Scar

A [[Highwallow]] spiritual organization that regards the Resonance Scar as sacred — a wound in the world that must not be healed but understood. They monitor Scar activity, help residents cope with its effects, and strongly oppose any Academy research that might destabilize it further.
