# Heartwood Staff

A staff carved from a species of tree that does not appear in any current taxonomy — the wood neither rots nor burns and is harder than most metals. Pre-Calamity manufacture. Currently in the Archive's physical artifact collection. Several [[Thyren]] have offered significant sums for it and been refused.

The staff stands approximately 180 centimeters tall, slightly curved, with a grain pattern that spirals along its length in a helix that the [[Duskfrost Academy]]'s botany department calls "biologically impossible" — no known tree species produces helical grain at this regularity. The wood's color shifts between deep amber and dark red depending on ambient temperature, a property the Academy initially attributed to a chemical coating before testing confirmed the color change occurs throughout the wood's entire cross-section. It responds to nothing: fire, acid, blade, saw, chisel, and three different Academy-approved destructive testing methods have all failed to mark it. A Tier 2 kinetic sigil directed at the staff was absorbed without visible effect. The staff was warm to the touch for approximately ten minutes afterward, which may indicate energy absorption or may indicate that the testing room was poorly ventilated.

The Thyren interest is the most telling detail. The [[Thyren]] have offered to purchase the staff on six separate occasions, each time increasing their bid — the most recent offer was four thousand crowns, an extraordinary sum for a wooden stick that does nothing obvious. When asked why they want it, Thyren representatives have provided explanations that are technically responsive and entirely uninformative: "cultural significance," "historical research," "aesthetic appreciation." The Academy's xenobiology department suspects the wood is from a tree species that the Thyren's own oral histories describe — a species that was, in Thyren ecological mythology, the first living thing to grow after the Calamity. If this is correct, the staff is not merely an artifact but a relic of a species the Thyren consider sacred. The Academy has not shared this theory with the Thyren, preferring to maintain its negotiating position.

It is stored alongside the [[Weight of Regret]] in the Archive's physical artifact collection, behind standard wards that [[Gorduin Wynlar]] has supplemented with additional protections after the most recent Thyren purchase attempt was accompanied by what the Archive's security team described as "an unusually thorough tour request" of the storage facilities. The staff produces no detectable arcane emissions, no resonance signature, and no measurable effects on its surroundings. It is, by every instrument the Academy possesses, an inert piece of wood that happens to be indestructible. Gorduin has observed that the pre-Calamity civilization built things to last, and that indestructibility was probably a mundane engineering specification rather than a remarkable property. The Academy disagrees. The staff remains in storage, doing nothing, while people argue about it. If it has an opinion on this arrangement, it has not expressed it.
