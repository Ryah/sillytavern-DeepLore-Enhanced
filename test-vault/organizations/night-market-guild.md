# Night Market Guild

The Night Market Guild is an informal trade organization managing the unlicensed night market in [[Amberburgh]]. Majority-[[Orvathi]] operated. The city watch maintains a deliberate blind spot about their operating license status in exchange for the market's cooperation with crime reporting.
