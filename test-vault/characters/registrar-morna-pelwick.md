# Registrar Morna Pelwick

Registrar at the [[Bellsummit]] Academy of Arcane Arts, responsible for enrollment records, researcher credentials, and access authorizations for the restricted collections. She has held this position for nineteen years and has developed a finely calibrated sense of when an application is legitimate and when someone is trying to get access to something they should not have. She has been wrong approximately twice.

Morna's office is the first room every student, researcher, and visiting scholar encounters at the Academy, and she has designed it to communicate exactly one message: nothing happens here without documentation. The desk faces the door. The filing cabinets are locked. The visitor's chair is comfortable enough for a short visit and uncomfortable enough to discourage a long one. She offers tea to people she likes and water to people she is evaluating.

Nineteen years of reading applications has given her an encyclopedic knowledge of who has studied what, under whom, and to what end — information that [[Professor Taevis Wrent]] occasionally tries to extract from her for departmental politics purposes, with limited success. She considers the enrollment records a sacred trust and the restricted collections access list a weapon that must be wielded with care.

What Morna does not discuss is that one of her two errors — the applications she misjudged — resulted in a researcher accessing materials that contributed to an incident the Academy has since classified. The incident report bears her signature alongside four others. She has not forgiven herself, and her subsequent caution is not professional temperament. It is penance.
