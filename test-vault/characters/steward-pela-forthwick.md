# Steward Pela Forthwick

Steward of the Triumvirate administrative compound, responsible for all domestic operations — staff, supply, maintenance, and the thousand daily decisions that keep a functioning seat of government operational. She has clearance to all non-restricted areas of the compound by virtue of her role, and encounters restricted information on a regular basis by virtue of being the person who organizes the rooms where it is discussed. She considers discretion a professional obligation rather than a personal choice.

Pela knows the compound better than the architects who designed it. She knows which corridors carry sound, which rooms have drafts that extinguish candles at the wrong moment, and which storage closets were converted from something else — knowledge she acquired through twenty-three years of managing a building that was never designed to be managed by one person. She has requested an assistant six times. The request has been approved four times and funded zero times.

The [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] considers her indispensable, which she finds professionally reassuring and personally exhausting. What exhausts her most is the information she absorbs incidentally — conversations overheard while replacing candles, documents left on tables during room turnovers, the patterns in who requests which meeting rooms and how often those requests change. She has never shared any of this. She has also never been able to forget it, and her dreams are increasingly full of connections she wishes she had not made.

She buys candles and wax supplies from [[Brix Hollowell]], with whom she shares a professional rapport built on the fact that they are both invisible people who keep visible institutions running. They have never discussed this explicitly. They do not need to.
