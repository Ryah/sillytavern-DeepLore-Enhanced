# Highwallow Night Clinic

A rotating unlicensed medical operation that runs out of three different Highwallow locations on alternating nights to avoid [[Wardenship]] attention. Staffed by two unlicensed practitioners, a retired Academy researcher, and whoever from the Mutual Aid Society can be spared. Charges what patients can afford, which is frequently nothing.
