# Founding of the Champions of Patience

The Champions of Patience were established in Year 34 by a coalition of former soldiers who refused to attach themselves to any Triumvirate house after the wars of consolidation ended. Their founding oath required service to the civilian population rather than to any political faction — a distinction that has kept them both respected and underfunded ever since.
