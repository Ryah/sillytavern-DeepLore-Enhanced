# Harbour Expansion Project

A fifteen-year construction project (Year 260-275) that doubled the harbor's berth capacity. Funded jointly by House Velkhast and the [[Amberburgh Merchants League]]. The expansion established Duskfrost as the dominant trade port on the northern coast.
