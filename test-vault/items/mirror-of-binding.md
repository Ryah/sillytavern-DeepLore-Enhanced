# Mirror of Binding

The Mirror of Binding is a standing mirror approximately two meters tall, framed in a material that absorbs light rather than reflecting it. Objects and living beings fully reflected in the mirror — fully, not partially — are trapped within the mirror's interior space, a pocket dimension with unclear properties.

The mirror currently contains four people and an unknown number of objects. Three of the people were trapped deliberately as a form of imprisonment. One was a researcher who slipped. All four are, as far as can be determined, alive — the mirror shows them moving, though they appear to be unaware of the exterior world.

Gorduin Wynlar maintains detailed records on the mirror's properties and has spent twenty years unsuccessfully attempting to extract the trapped researcher. The extraction problem is that anything used to reach into the mirror becomes subject to the same reflection rules.

The mirror was constructed during the Reconstruction era by a practitioner whose name does not appear in any record, suggesting either deliberate erasure or that the name was never recorded. Its original purpose is unknown. "Trap living beings" is considered an unlikely intended use by most researchers, who prefer to believe it was designed for something more benign that went wrong.

This item has significance in [[Bellsummit]].
