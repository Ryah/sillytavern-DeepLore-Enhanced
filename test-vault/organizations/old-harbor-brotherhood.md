# Old Harbor Brotherhood

The Old Harbor Brotherhood predates the [[Bonehands]] by sixty years. They once controlled the harbor district entirely. Now they hold perhaps two square blocks of territory and maintain it through a combination of stubbornness and the fact that the Bonehands find eliminating them more trouble than it's worth.
