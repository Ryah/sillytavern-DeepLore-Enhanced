# Yseult Marchant

Human luxury goods trader based in [[Amberburgh]]'s upper commercial district, specializing in imported textiles, rare pigments, and the kind of goods that require connections rather than just capital to source. She cultivates relationships with foreign trading houses that most Amberburgh merchants cannot access, which makes her goods genuinely rare and her social calendar full. She is a reliable source of information about what wealthy people are currently buying, which is often more interesting than it sounds.
