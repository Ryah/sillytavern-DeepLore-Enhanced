# Scar Dweller Testimonies

People who live adjacent to the Resonance Scar report consistent effects: tools occasionally refuse to function, dreams near the boundary are unusually vivid, and animals avoid the area. Children born in the Scar-adjacent tenements show slightly elevated arcane affinity rates. The Academy considers this data; the residents consider it their landlords' problem.

Most documented testimonies come from the eastern districts closest to [[Bellsummit]].
