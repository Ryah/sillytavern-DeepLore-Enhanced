# Nightwatch Protocol

The Nightwatch Protocol governs [[Scorchhelm]]'s after-dark patrol rotation — four overlapping shifts, each responsible for specific districts and gate positions. The protocol was established after the Third Consolidation when a single unguarded night allowed a rival faction to plant incendiaries in the lower armory. The garrison has not had a fully unguarded night since.

Scholars connect this to the [[War of Glimmering Hope]].
