# Nightmarket Customs

The [[Amberburgh]] night market operates under a parallel set of customs distinct from daytime trade law. The key conventions: no questions about provenance, no Triumvirate insignia displayed, and the first offer is never the real offer. [[Wardenship]] patrols exist but maintain deliberate blindness to most transactions. The night market has survived four official attempts to close it; the fifth attempt is currently stalled in committee.

This concept is closely tied to the [[Resonance Frequency Theory]].
