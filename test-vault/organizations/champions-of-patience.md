# Champions of Patience

The Champions of Patience is a knightly order founded during the Reconstruction era, originally tasked with protecting civilian populations during the chaotic decades following the Calamity. Three centuries later, they have evolved into a prestigious but politically marginalized institution — respected, modestly funded, and thoroughly outmaneuvered by everyone with actual power.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: Knightly Order | Founded: Year 34 | Location: [[Scorchhelm]] barracks | Commander: High Champion Mira Veldthar | Members: ~80 active | Motto: "We wait; they need not"]
</div>

The Champions operate under a strict code that prohibits accepting payment for protection, engaging in commerce, or swearing loyalty to any Triumvirate house. This code, designed to ensure neutrality, has left them perpetually underfunded and dependent on Academy stipends and public donations.

Their training emphasizes defense over offense — the order produces exceptional bodyguards and garrison soldiers but relatively few battlefield commanders. The motto "We wait; they need not" reflects their foundational philosophy: a Champion takes a blow so a civilian does not.

**Current status:** The order is at its lowest membership since Year 180. Young people with martial ambition increasingly join the [[Solarguard]] for better pay or pursue mercenary work. The Champions are not dying — but they are declining.

**Notable members:** [[Tybellan Hercyne]] (field agent, atypical), High Champion Mira Veldthar (administrator and last true believer).
