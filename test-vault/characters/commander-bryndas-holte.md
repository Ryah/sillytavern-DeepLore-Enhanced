# Commander Bryndas Holte

[[Solarguard]] commander at [[Scorchhelm]], responsible for regional military operations across the eastern Duskfrost approaches. He is a career officer who rose through the ranks on demonstrated competence rather than political connection — a rarity that has made him both respected and occasionally inconvenient to his superiors. He maintains the [[Compact Vault]] access protocols personally and has declined three requests from [[Gorduin Wynlar]] to share copies of the documents therein.
