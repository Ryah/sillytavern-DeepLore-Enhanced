# Delivese

The Delivese are tall, pale-skinned humanoids who thrive in temperatures that would send most species indoors. Roughly 600 live in Duskfrost, the vast majority settled in [[Rimeborough]], where the cold sea winds off the northern coast maintain year-round conditions that other species endure only when they must. For the Delivese, Rimeborough is not a hardship posting. It is the only district in the city that feels like home.

Delivese physiology runs cold. Their resting body temperature sits well below what a [[Human]] physician would consider survivable, and prolonged exposure to summer heat in the lower districts leaves them sluggish, disoriented, and prone to a condition they call *thaw-sick* — essentially heatstroke at temperatures most species find merely warm. This biological constraint shapes their entire relationship with the city: Delivese rarely venture south of [[Rimeborough]] between late spring and early autumn, and those who must work in warmer districts carry cooling stones — small enchanted blocks commissioned from the Academy at considerable expense.

Their culture is built around patience and precision. Delivese artisans produce Duskfrost's finest cold-forged metalwork — blades, surgical instruments, and delicate clockwork mechanisms worked at temperatures that would make other smiths' hands seize. The [[Rimeborough Market]] is the only reliable source for Delivese steel, which commands premium prices throughout the city. Their tradition holds that metal worked in warmth is "nervous" — prone to stress fractures and imprecision — while cold-forged metal is "settled." Metallurgists at the Academy have tested this claim and found it, annoyingly, largely correct.

Delivese social structure is formal and unhurried. Conversations are long. Decisions are slow. Commitments, once made, are treated as permanent. This makes them frustrating trade partners for the [[Bolludine]] and excellent ones for the [[Wardenship]], which values their methodical approach to contract fulfillment. Several Delivese serve as arbitrators in commercial disputes, their reputation for dispassionate judgment well-earned if somewhat literal — it is difficult to be emotionally volatile when your biology runs at half-speed in every heated room.

The [[Triumvirate]] largely leaves Rimeborough to self-govern, a concession born less from respect than from the practical reality that no one else wants to administer a district where the pipes freeze six months of the year. The Delivese accept this neglect with characteristic equanimity. They were here before the current Triumvirate, and they expect to be here after it.
