# Sigil Theory — Fundamentals

Sigils function as arcane resonators — geometric patterns that establish a specific harmonic relationship with ambient arcane energy and channel it toward a defined effect. The geometry is not arbitrary: every angle and curve in a working sigil corresponds to a specific resonance frequency. Errors produce failure or, at high energy levels, catastrophic results.

Foundational coursework at the [[Duskfrost Academy]] covers this material in the first year.
