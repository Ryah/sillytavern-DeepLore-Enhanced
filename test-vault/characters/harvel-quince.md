# Harvel Quince

Human debt collector for the [[Bonehands]] in [[Bayside Shushail]] — the person who visits when payments are overdue and has a conversation about the importance of keeping agreements. He is not violent unless the conversation exhausts other options, which he considers a professional standard rather than a moral position. He keeps detailed notes on every account and has never lost a debt to statute of limitations.
