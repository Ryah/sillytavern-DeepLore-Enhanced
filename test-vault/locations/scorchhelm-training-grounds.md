# Scorchhelm Training Grounds

The [[Solarguard]] maintains a walled training complex in Scorchhelm's northern quarter — basic weapon drill, formation practice, and the new arcane sensitivity exercises the [[Wardenship]] liaison program requires. Open to active officers only. [[Champions of Patience]] have a smaller separate facility.

Shares administrative ties with [[East Hamp District]].
