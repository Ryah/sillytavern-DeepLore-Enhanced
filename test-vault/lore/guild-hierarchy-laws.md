# Guild Hierarchy Laws

Duskfrost recognizes four guild tiers: Licensed, Registered, Sponsored, and Charter. Charter guilds have independent legal standing and can bring suit in Triumvirate courts directly. Sponsored guilds require a Charter guild patron. Disputes between guilds of different tiers are adjudicated by the Commerce Board, which is widely regarded as impartial by Charter guilds and as owned by Charter guilds by everyone else.

This concept is closely tied to the [[War of Glimmering Hope]].
