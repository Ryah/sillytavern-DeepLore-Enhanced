# Champions Chapel

A small, austere chapel within the [[Champions of Patience]] barracks complex in [[Scorchhelm]]. Used for investiture ceremonies, renewal of oaths, and the daily contemplative practice the order requires of active members. Non-members are permitted entry only by invitation from a Knight Exemplar or above.

The chapel is a single rectangular room of unadorned grey stone, thirty paces long and half as wide, lit by narrow windows set high in the walls that admit thin shafts of light but no view of the outside. There is no altar in the conventional sense — only a flat stone at the far end, waist-high, on which the order's founding oath is carved in letters deep enough to cast shadows. The benches are stone, without cushions. The floor is bare. The temperature inside is always slightly colder than the corridor outside, an effect the order attributes to the building's age and visiting Academy researchers attribute to the density of the stonework. The silence in the chapel is not the passive quiet of an empty room but the active, weighted silence of a space designed to press against the mind.

During investiture ceremonies, the chapel fills to its modest capacity of forty. The candidate kneels on the bare stone before the oath-slab while the Knight Commander speaks the words that have not changed in two centuries. The ceremony is short — the order has no patience for theatrical ritual — but the weight of it is evident in the faces of those present. The air smells of cold stone and the faint residual incense that the order burns once weekly, a resinous, bitter blend that lingers in the pores of the walls for days.

Outside of ceremonies, the chapel serves as the order's contemplative space. Members arrive alone, usually before dawn, and sit in the cold silence for precisely one hour. The practice is mandatory for active knights and voluntary for retired ones. What the contemplation involves — whether prayer, meditation, or simply the discipline of stillness — the order does not discuss with outsiders. The [[Solarguard]] officers who share the [[Scorchhelm]] complex have learned not to enter the chapel corridor during the pre-dawn hours, not because they have been forbidden, but because the quality of the silence discourages interruption.
