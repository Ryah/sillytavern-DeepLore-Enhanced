# Elemental Band

A simple metal bracelet of pre-Calamity manufacture that grants the wearer resistance to minor elemental effects — light rain passes around them, small fires do not ignite their clothing, and minor cold does not penetrate. Provides no protection against significant elemental forces. Currently in the [[Amberburgh Merchants League]] auction house pending sale.
