# Duskfrost Food Economy

Duskfrost's food supply depends on three sources, controlled by three different power structures, and distributed with an equity that precisely reflects the city's political priorities — which is to say, unevenly.

**Grain:** The [[Harcyne Granary Authority]] controls grain storage and distribution from the southern lowland farms. House Harcyne sets the prices, manages the supply chain, and maintains granary infrastructure. They do not officially create shortages. They do occasionally delay shipments during political negotiations, which has the same effect and the advantage of deniability. A standard grain ration costs two copper bits per day in [[Amberburgh]]. The same ration costs three in [[Highwallow]], because the distribution wagons charge a "marsh premium" for the road conditions. The wagons are Harcyne-contracted.

**Fish:** The harbor fishing fleet operates under the [[Redpond Fishers]] guild and independent operators in [[Bayside Shushail]]. Fish is the most accessible protein for Duskfrost's lower classes — fresh at the waterfront, preserved inland. The [[Zuvine]] contribute to the catch through their underwater foraging operations, though their relationship with the surface fishing fleet is competitive rather than cooperative. Harbor fish prices are set by daily auction at the [[Redpond Waterfront]]. The Bonehands take a cut.

**Root vegetables and greens:** Cultivated in the marsh gardens east of Highwallow, primarily by [[Thyren]] farmers using growth-enhancement affinities. These gardens feed the lower districts and produce surplus for the Amberburgh markets. They are vulnerable to flooding, Scar turbulence, and the periodic decisions of the Triumvirate to redirect water management resources to more politically important districts.

**Meat:** Scarce and expensive. Limited livestock grazing land means that beef and pork are [[Bellsummit]] luxuries. Poultry is available in Amberburgh at a price. Highwallow residents eat meat when they can afford it, which is rarely.

**District access:** Bellsummit eats well — imported spices, fresh catches, white bread, wine. Amberburgh eats adequately — market food, grain bread, ale, seasonal variety. Highwallow eats what it can get — preserved fish, dense bread, marsh vegetables, and whatever the [[Highwallow Mutual Aid]] hall distributes on bad weeks. The gradient is visible, predictable, and exactly as the Triumvirate designed it.

The political reality is simple: whoever controls the grain controls the city's political baseline. Harcyne knows this. The other houses know this. The people eating two-copper bread know this. Nobody has successfully challenged it because the alternative — Harcyne deciding not to distribute grain at all — is worse than any price.
