# Resonance Scar Properties

The Resonance Scar is a persistent zone of arcane turbulence in [[Highwallow]], a direct consequence of the Calamity's grid collapse. Sigils drawn in the Scar zone have a measurable (approximately 12%) chance of behaving unexpectedly. The boundary fluctuates seasonally. The Academy has been mapping it since Year 198 with incomplete results.

This concept is closely tied to the [[War of Glimmering Hope]].
