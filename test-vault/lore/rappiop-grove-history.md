# Rappiop Grove History

Rappiop Grove was established as a botanical research garden by the Academy in Year 180. For 132 years it was exactly what it appeared to be. The pre-Calamity resonance grid fragment beneath it was discovered during routine foundation inspection in Year 312 — the discovery that precipitated the current political crisis. The grove is now under joint Academy-Triumvirate control. The original botanical research was discontinued and no one has watered the specimens.

Scholars connect this to the [[Oracles Summit Protocol]].
