# First Triumvirate Compact

Signed in Year 23, the First Triumvirate Compact formalized the power-sharing arrangement between the three dominant families. It has been amended fourteen times. Legal scholars argue about which amendments supersede which. The current document is forty-three pages long and internally inconsistent in at least three places.

Later superseded in scope by the [[Binding Compact]], though certain original clauses remain legally binding.
