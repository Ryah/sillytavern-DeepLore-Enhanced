Welcome to DeepLore Enhanced

Your Obsidian vault, wired into SillyTavern.

What is this?

DeepLore Enhanced connects your Obsidian lore vault to SillyTavern, automatically injecting relevant worldbuilding entries into your roleplay context. Your characters, locations, lore, and rules — pulled in when they matter.

How does it work?

  1. Keywords in chat text match against your vault entries
  2. AI refinement (optional) picks the most relevant matches
  3. Entries are injected into the AI's context window automatically

This wizard will walk you through the full setup. You can change everything later in Settings.

Librarian

Let the AI search for and flag missing lore during generation.

What is the Librarian?

The Librarian gives your writing AI two optional tools during generation: search (look up vault entries the pipeline missed) and flag (note a gap in your lore for later). Flagged gaps appear in the drawer's Librarian tab, where you can review them and use an AI-assisted editor to create new entries.

This is completely optional and off by default. Most generations should have zero tool calls if your vault is well-maintained.

Token Cost

Enabling the Librarian increases token usage. Tool definitions add ~300-500 tokens to every generation's system prompt. Each search call adds a round-trip to the generation (the model pauses, searches, then continues). Flag calls are cheaper (no round-trip results). Start with just the flag tool if you want to minimize cost and see what your AI reaches for before enabling search.

Requires a model that supports function calling (tool use).
