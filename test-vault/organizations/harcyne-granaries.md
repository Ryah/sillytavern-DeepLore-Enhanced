# Harcyne Granary Authority

The Harcyne Granary Authority controls food storage and distribution for Duskfrost and the surrounding agricultural regions. Their monopoly on grain storage gives [[Duskfrost Triumvirate|House Harcyne]] a political lever no other house can match — they can cause food shortages without technically doing anything illegal.
