# Crennish

Crennish are small (averaging 4'0") humanoids with photographic memory. The Archive employs several as cataloguers. Their reproduction rate — three to four children per year is typical — means their population in Duskfrost has grown significantly in the past two decades.

Their photographic memory makes them valuable to the [[Orvathi]], who hire Crennish translators for complex multi-party negotiations.
