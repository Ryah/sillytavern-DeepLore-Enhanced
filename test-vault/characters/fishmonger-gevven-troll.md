# Gevven Troll

Fishmonger in [[Bayside Shushail]]'s morning market, present every day before dawn and departed before midday. He is a neighborhood fixture — knows every face, every regular customer, every subtle shift in what the district is doing and who is doing it. He does not sell this information, but he does mention things in conversation, which is a distinction that makes him comfortable and keeps him safe. He has been doing this for thirty years and intends to do it for thirty more.
