# Triumvirate Secret Sessions

Beyond the public council record, the three Triumvirate houses maintain a parallel meeting practice: closed sessions with no official transcript, held in a rotating venue not disclosed to staff. The practice is technically legal under the charter but politically contentious. An estimated 40% of actual governance decisions are made in closed session; the public record ratifies the results.

These sessions are the [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]]' s most closely guarded institutional practice.
