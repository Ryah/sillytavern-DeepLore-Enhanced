# Highwallow Mutual Aid Society

The Highwallow Mutual Aid Society provides food, shelter mediation, and basic medical services to Highwallow's most vulnerable residents, funded entirely by donations and small fees from those who can afford them. It operates in a building that is technically condemned and chooses not to think too hard about that.
