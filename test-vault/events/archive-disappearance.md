# Archive Volume Disappearance

Eight years ago, three volumes vanished from the Archive's restricted collection without any record of checkout. The subsequent two-year investigation implicated seventeen people and charged none. The volumes — a necromantic theory text, pre-Calamity correspondence, and an untitled volume with a silver eye on its cover — have not been found.

[[Gorduin Wynlar]] was consulted during the investigation and remains unsatisfied with its conclusions.
