# Truth Vial

An alchemical compound produced by the [[Duskfrost Academy]] that compels truthful responses for approximately one hour after ingestion. Legally restricted. Requires Academy permit to purchase. Widely available on the [[Bonehands]] black market for twice the licensed price.

The compound is a clear, slightly viscous liquid with a faint metallic taste that subjects describe as "unpleasant but not unbearable." Its mechanism is arcano-chemical: the active ingredients create a temporary resonance pattern in the subject's nervous system that interferes with the cognitive process of constructing false statements. The subject can think lies, plan lies, and intend to lie — they simply cannot vocalize them. The words come out true instead, often to the subject's visible distress. The compulsion does not extend to written communication, gestures, or silence; a subject under Truth Vial influence can refuse to speak entirely, which is why experienced interrogators pair it with conventional pressure techniques. The compound has no effect on statements the subject genuinely believes to be true, regardless of their actual accuracy — a person who is sincerely wrong will state their incorrect belief with full confidence.

The Academy produces Truth Vial in controlled batches of fifty units per quarter, each vial individually serialized and tracked through a chain of custody that the Commerce Board audits annually. Licensed purchasers include the [[Wardenship]] (the largest customer), the [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]]'s judicial branch, and a small number of private investigators who have obtained the necessary permits. The permit process requires a stated purpose, a licensed administrator (the vial must be given by a trained professional, not self-administered), and a signed consent form from the subject — this last requirement being the legal fiction that makes the entire system work, since a subject who consents to truth compulsion while in Wardenship custody has usually been presented with alternatives that make consent the rational choice.

The Bonehands' black market supply is chemically identical to the Academy's product, which means either the Bonehands have a source inside the Academy's production facility or they have independently replicated the formula. The Academy officially maintains that independent replication is impossible given the complexity of the manufacturing process. The Academy's security department unofficially maintains a list of production staff who have recently purchased property beyond their salary range. The black market price — roughly eight crowns per vial, compared to four crowns through licensed channels — reflects the convenience premium of avoiding the permit process, the consent requirement, and the paper trail. The Bonehands guarantee discretion. The [[Sword of Enigmas]] achieves the same truth-compelling effect without chemicals, but one cannot slip a sword into someone's drink at a tavern.
