# Highwallow Underground Economy

Highwallow hosts a thriving informal economy in arcane services that the [[Wardenship]] is technically required to suppress and practically unable to. Unlicensed practitioners offer diagnosis, minor warding, and sigil work at rates the licensed Academy clinics cannot match. The Scar's interference makes accurate measurement difficult, providing convenient deniability for all parties. The Wardenship makes approximately three dozen arrests per year; the number of active unlicensed practitioners is estimated in the hundreds.
