# Hell's Statue

A half-meter obsidian statue of unclear subject that radiates low-level infernal energy detectable to [[Selaveth]] and sensitive arcane instruments. Recovered from the deep [[Highwallow]] tunnels. Currently sealed in a lead-lined container in the Archive's restricted storage. The Academy is not sure what to do with it.
