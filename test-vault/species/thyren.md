# Thyren

Thyren are humanoids with a symbiotic relationship with plant life — their touch encourages accelerated growth in compatible species. Several Thyren maintain the living hedges and tree-arches of [[Rappiop Grove]] under contract with the Academy.

Their biological affinity for plant growth is of particular interest to [[Bhissalae]] researchers studying cross-species symbiosis.
