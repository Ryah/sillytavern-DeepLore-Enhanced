# East Hamp District

East Hamp is a working-class residential district of dense housing, communal wells, and three markets that serve the district's significant [[Cirilae]] and [[Vorruk]] populations. The [[Marblesmiths]] guild recruits heavily here.

Shares administrative ties with [[Bellsummit]].
