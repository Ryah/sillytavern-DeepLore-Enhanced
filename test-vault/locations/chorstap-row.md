# Chorstap Row

A market street in western [[Amberburgh]] specializing in imported and unusual goods — spices from the [[Kethvali]] caravans, [[Zuvine]] handicrafts, Academy-certified arcane components at retail prices. Busy during morning and midday, closes by late afternoon.

Trade routes connect this area to [[Bolludine Quarter]].
