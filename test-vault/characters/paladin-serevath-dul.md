# Paladin Serevath Dul

Senior field officer of the [[Champions of Patience]], an elf paladin whose title undersells both her age (247) and her combat experience. The Champions of Patience operate on the principle that righteous action taken at the wrong moment is less valuable than righteous action deferred until conditions are correct. Serevath has internalized this completely, which makes her devastating in combat — she has been waiting for the right moment for two centuries and she recognizes it when it arrives.
