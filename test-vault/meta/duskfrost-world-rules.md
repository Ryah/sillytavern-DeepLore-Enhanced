# Duskfrost World Rules

Duskfrost is a sprawling fantasy city built on the ruins of a pre-Calamity civilization. Magic is regulated but not rare. The city is governed by the Triumvirate — three noble houses that share power in a shifting balance of alliances and rivalries.

**Tone:** Gritty urban fantasy. Political intrigue, faction conflict, underground economies. Magic has costs. Nothing is entirely good or evil.

**Technology level:** Late medieval. No gunpowder. Arcane machinery exists but is expensive and rare.

**Geography:** Built on a coastal bluff above a natural harbor. Districts range from the wealthy [[Bellsummit]] heights to the flooded, crime-ridden [[Highwallow]] marshes below.

**Magic:** Channeled through sigils, binding contracts, and bloodline affinities. Wild magic exists but is stigmatized. The Academy regulates arcane practice within city limits.

The [[Duskfrost Academy]] is the primary institution for arcane education and research within the city.
