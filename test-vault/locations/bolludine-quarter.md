# Bolludine Quarter

Three adjacent streets in central [[Amberburgh]] where the three major Bolludine shard networks maintain their public-facing operations. Couriers arrive and depart continuously. The information exchange that happens here is the informal backbone of Duskfrost's commercial intelligence system.

Shares administrative ties with [[Bellsummit]].
