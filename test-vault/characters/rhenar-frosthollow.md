# Rhenar Frosthollow

Rhenar Frosthollow leads the [[Rimeborough]] district in the way that most Duskfrost communities are actually led: not by election or appointment, but by being the person who shows up to every meeting, remembers every promise, and does the work no one else volunteers for. The Triumvirate has no formal relationship with Rimeborough's governance. Rhenar is Rimeborough's governance.

The [[Delivese]] settled Rimeborough because no one else wanted it. The northern clifftop's persistent sea winds maintain temperatures that most species find miserable, which made the real estate affordable and the neighbors few. Three generations later, the district is quiet, clean, and self-sufficient in a way that makes the Triumvirate slightly nervous — self-sufficient communities do not need the Triumvirate, and communities that do not need the Triumvirate tend to have opinions about whether it should continue to exist.

Rhenar manages the district's internal affairs with the administrative precision of someone who grew up watching the [[Bellsummit]] bureaucracy from a distance and decided he could do it better with fewer people. He mediates disputes, maintains the district's modest infrastructure, coordinates with the [[Rimeborough Market]] vendors, and represents the community's interests in the rare interactions with the [[Solarguard]] — who patrol Rimeborough infrequently and carefully, because the cold makes their shift rotations complicated and because the district's crime rate is the lowest in the city.

His politics are straightforwardly isolationist. He wants Rimeborough left alone. He considers the [[Velkhast Succession Crisis]] a problem for people who depend on Velkhast trade routes, which Rimeborough largely does not. He considers the Academy's expansion into the northern districts a potential threat to the community's autonomy. He has expressed these views clearly, politely, and with the understated certainty of a man who knows that the cold will outlast the politics.

He maintains a functional relationship with [[Barlyn Greymantle]] through the Amberburgh council, where Rimeborough's interests occasionally overlap with the [[Marblesmiths]]' construction concerns. The two share a preference for practical solutions and a distaste for meetings that could have been messages.
