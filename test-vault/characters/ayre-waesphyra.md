# Ayre Waesphyra

Ayre Waesphyra has been studying the Antecedent Culture for ninety years, which is longer than most humans live and represents, she estimates, approximately twelve percent of the total time that will be required to understand it properly. She is unhurried. She has context.

Ayre produced the Waesphyra Translation — the most comprehensive modern rendering of [[Antecedent Script]] after Pallasys, covering approximately 68% of known characters versus Pallasys's 60%. She considers this incomplete. She is working on the remaining 32% and has been for twenty years.

Her recent translation of Scribe Fragment 7-C is the most significant piece of primary source research the Oracles have published in a decade. It is also, she notes with characteristic understatement, "somewhat troubling."

She shares a productive working relationship with [[Gorduin Wynlar]] based on mutual competence and the understanding that neither will make dramatic decisions without consulting the other first. This agreement has been violated twice, both times by her, both times because the situation did not allow for consultation.
