# Quirkavel Doss

Quirkavel Doss talks faster than most people think, which is the point. She runs the largest of the three [[Bolludine]] shard networks in [[Amberburgh]] — the Doss shard, named for her grandmother, who established it during the Reconstruction era when reliable message delivery was the difference between a functioning trade route and a dead one. Three generations later, the shard handles an estimated forty percent of Amberburgh's non-official message traffic.

Her compound eyes track six conversations simultaneously in the courier office — she has been observed giving instructions to three runners, negotiating a price with a client, monitoring the door, and eating lunch at the same time. Non-Bolludine visitors to the Quarter find this unnerving. Bolludine visitors find it ordinary.

The Doss shard's real value is not speed — all three shards are fast. It is accuracy. Quirkavel's grandmother established a policy of triple-verification for any message involving numbers or names, and the shard has maintained a 99.7% accuracy rate for thirty years. This makes the Doss shard the preferred network for [[Velkhast Trading Company|Velkhast Trading]] contracts, [[Marblesmiths]] guild communications, and anyone else who needs to know that the message received is the message sent.

She keeps meticulous records. Every message logged, every courier route timed, every client interaction documented in a shorthand that only Doss shard members can read. She has been approached by the [[Solarguard]] four times for access to these records. She has declined four times, citing client confidentiality. The Solarguard has not pressed the issue, which suggests they already get what they need from the other two shards.

Her relationship with [[Grolgrurim Bluntgut]] is one of mutual professional respect — he uses the Doss shard exclusively, pays promptly, and does not attempt to negotiate the rates. She considers him the only non-Bolludine in Amberburgh who genuinely understands the value of accurate information delivery.
