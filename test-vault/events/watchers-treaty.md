# Watchers Treaty

An informal (and therefore deniable) agreement reached in Year 301 between [[Solarguard]] Commander Elia Vayne and [[Bonehands]] leader Thick Margol. The treaty established current territorial boundaries: Solarguard primary in upper districts, Bonehands primary in the harbor and marsh. Both sides have violated the treaty repeatedly and both sides pretend it does not exist.

The treaty was negotiated in the aftermath of the [[Battle of the Vanguard Crossing]], which demonstrated that open conflict benefited neither side.
