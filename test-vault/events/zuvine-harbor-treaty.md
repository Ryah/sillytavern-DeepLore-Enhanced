# Zuvine Harbor Treaty

Year 189. Following two decades of disputes over salvage rights and surface residency, the Triumvirate negotiated a formal treaty with Zuvine community leaders. The treaty granted Zuvine communities legal personhood, surface residency rights, and exclusive salvage licenses for the inner harbor. In exchange, the Zuvine shared navigational charts of the underwater cave networks. The Academy added an undisclosed rider to the treaty that Zuvine community leaders have never publicly discussed.
