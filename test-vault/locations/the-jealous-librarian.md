# The Jealous Librarian

A faculty tavern adjacent to the Academy in [[Bellsummit]]. The name refers to the Archive Head Archivist's rumored opinion of the [[Oracles of Might]]'s private collection. Serves decent wine at high prices. The primary venue for academic gossip, hiring negotiations, and post-seminar arguments.
