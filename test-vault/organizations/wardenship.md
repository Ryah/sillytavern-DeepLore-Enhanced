# Wardenship

The Academy's enforcement arm, with jurisdiction over registered arcane practitioners anywhere in Duskfrost. Small (22 wardens), but each is an accomplished practitioner. They operate parallel to the [[Solarguard]] with deliberately ambiguous overlap jurisdiction.
