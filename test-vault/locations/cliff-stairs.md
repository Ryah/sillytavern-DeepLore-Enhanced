# Cliffside Stairs

A public stairway cut into the Duskfrost bluff connecting [[Bellsummit]] to the mid-cliff districts below. Technically maintained by the city, practically maintained by whoever feels like it. The lifts are monitored. The stairs are not. Anyone who uses the stairs instead of the lifts has a reason to prefer not being observed, and everyone on the stairs understands this about everyone else.
