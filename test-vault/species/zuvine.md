# Zuvine

Zuvines are a species of semi-aquatic humanoids with bioluminescent markings along their arms, jaw, and upper chest. They are native to the underwater cave networks beneath the Duskfrost harbor, with a population of approximately 400 living in the city above the waterline and an unknown number remaining below.

Zuvines are capable of breathing both air and water. In water, their bioluminescent markings activate, pulsing with their emotional state — an involuntary biological response that makes Zuvines extremely poor liars in underwater conversation. Above water, markings are dim and mostly static.

Zuvines in Duskfrost primarily work in the harbor trade, fishing industry, and as licensed dive salvagers. Their access to the underwater cave systems makes them invaluable for recovering pre-Calamity artifacts from the submerged ruins below the harbor. The Academy pays well for this work.

Their culture emphasizes collective memory through oral tradition — complex song-cycles that encode history, law, and genealogy. Zuvine elders serve as living archives. The loss of an elder to violence or accident is treated as equivalent to the destruction of a library.

Relations with surface species are functional but cool. Zuvines remember that the Calamity destroyed their coastal settlements as thoroughly as it destroyed the city above, and that the reconstruction period focused entirely on the surface population's needs.

The [[Thyren]] who maintain the harbor-side gardens have developed a working relationship with Zuvine foragers, exchanging cultivated algae for fresh-water specimens.
