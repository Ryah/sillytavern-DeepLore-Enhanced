# Theories of the Calamity

Three major theories compete to explain the Calamity. The accident theory holds it was a cascading engineering failure. The attack theory holds it was a deliberate weapon. The transcendence theory holds it was intentional — that the Antecedent Culture triggered the Calamity deliberately to achieve something they considered worth the cost. The Scribe Fragment (if authentic) supports none of them cleanly and all of them partially.

The [[Oracles of Might]] archive holds the most complete collection of competing theoretical frameworks.
