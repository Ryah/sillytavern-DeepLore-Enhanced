# The Calamity Onset

Year Zero. The resonance tower network failed in a cascade that lasted approximately six hours from first failure to total collapse. The energy release reshaped the harbor, destroyed approximately two-thirds of the existing city, and created the Resonance Scar in what is now [[Highwallow]]. Reliable records from the period do not exist; what is known comes from archaeology and the fragments Thornwick Pallasys translated from pre-Calamity sources.
