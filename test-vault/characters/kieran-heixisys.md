# Kieran Heixisys

Junior lecturer at the [[Bellsummit]] Academy of Arcane Arts, responsible for first-year instruction in foundational theory. He is younger than most of his students expect an elf academic to be — only 89 — and compensates with exceptional preparation. His notes on Duskfrost elemental interaction are used by three senior researchers who have never acknowledged this.

Maintains a professional relationship with [[Sarros Sylren]].
