# Academy Fire of Year 211

A catastrophic fire destroyed the Academy's original east wing in Year 211, consuming an estimated four thousand volumes. The cause remains officially undetermined. Several scholars believe the fire was deliberately set to destroy specific texts before they could be studied.

The fire may have been set to prevent discovery of what later became the [[Resonance Grid Discovery]].
