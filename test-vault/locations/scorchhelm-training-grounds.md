# Scorchhelm Training Grounds

The [[Solarguard]] maintains a walled training complex in [[Scorchhelm]]'s northern quarter — basic weapon drill, formation practice, and the new arcane sensitivity exercises the [[Wardenship]] liaison program requires. Open to active officers only. [[Champions of Patience]] have a smaller separate facility behind the eastern wall, visible but pointedly separate.

The grounds are a rectangle of packed earth and sand, roughly two hundred paces long, enclosed by walls high enough to block the wind but not the sound. From dawn to late afternoon, the complex rings with the crack of wooden practice weapons, shouted cadence counts, and the particular dull thud of a body hitting dirt. The air smells of sweat, leather oil, and the medicinal salve the quartermaster distributes for bruises — a sharp, camphor-heavy ointment that has not changed its recipe in decades. Sand pits line the western edge for grappling practice, their surfaces raked smooth each morning and churned to chaos by noon.

Recruits train in the open yard under the supervision of drill instructors who have elevated criticism to an art form. Weapon racks along the north wall hold practice swords, spears, shields, and the weighted training clubs used for conditioning. The equipment is maintained to a standard that the actual patrol armory does not always match, a fact the drill instructors find professionally embarrassing. The arcane sensitivity exercises — a recent addition mandated by the [[Wardenship]] — take place in a cordoned section of the eastern yard, where officers practice detecting and responding to low-level arcane effects under controlled conditions. Most officers approach these sessions with visible skepticism. The instructors have learned not to argue the point.

The Champions' facility beyond the eastern wall is smaller but better equipped, with reinforced sparring rings and a meditation yard of raked gravel. The two facilities share a wall but not a gate. When Solarguard recruits glance over the wall at the Champions training, they see exercises performed with a precision and quiet discipline that makes their own drills look like tavern brawls. The Champions, for their part, do not look back.
