# Grolgrurim Bluntgut

Grolgrurim Bluntgut has been the commercial driving force behind the [[Marblesmiths]] guild for forty years, though he holds no formal title — his actual title is "Senior Trading Partner," which he chose himself and which accurately describes his function and nothing else. He owns significant real estate in [[Amberburgh]], maintains trade relationships with eight regional partners, and is pursuing a four thousand gold crown claim against a dark elf information broker that the Triumvirate courts have declined to hear twice.

Grolgrurim is blunt (the family name is occupational history — early ancestors made blunt instruments) in his speech and meticulous in his contracts. The four-thousand-crown dispute with [[Zuni Dhegevnac]] is not primarily about the money at this point — it is about the principle, which is that contracts mean what they say and not what one party subsequently claims they mean.

He sits on the Amberburgh district council as a representative of the Marblesmiths interests and participates in Triumvirate trade negotiations as an industry advisor. In both contexts he is effective, specific, and generally irritating to the people who prefer vague agreements.
