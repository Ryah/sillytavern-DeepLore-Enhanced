# Great Flood of Highwallow

In Year 234, a combination of sustained rainfall and Scar-zone ground subsidence caused catastrophic flooding in Highwallow. Approximately four hundred residents died. Thousands were displaced. The Triumvirate's response — inadequate and delayed — significantly damaged their already-poor relationship with the district's population.
