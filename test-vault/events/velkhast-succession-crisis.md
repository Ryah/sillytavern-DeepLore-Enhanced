# Velkhast Succession Crisis

The death of [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] head Aldric Velkhast without a designated heir has created a succession dispute now in its second year. Three claimants are actively competing. The [[Solarguard]] has aligned with the second. The [[Bonehands]] with the third. The first claimant disappeared eight months ago.

The crisis escalated dramatically during the [[Night of Empty Chairs]].
