# Felicitas Langguth

Felicitas Langguth holds the title of Senior City Assessor for the [[Amberburgh]] and [[Bayside Shushail]] districts, which means she is responsible for property valuation, trade license compliance, and the various forms of administrative oversight that make commerce possible and occasionally inconvenient. What the title does not capture is that she is also the most effective informal intelligence conduit between the Triumvirate's administrative apparatus and the [[Oracles of Might]].

Felicitas is not technically a spy. She is an assessor who happens to encounter a great deal of sensitive information in the course of legitimate administrative work, who has cultivated relationships with people in multiple institutional positions, and who considers it professional courtesy to share information that might be useful — provided the sharing is reciprocal.

Her regular Monday/Thursday reservations at the [[The Hidden Dragonfruit Tavern]] are the primary venue for most of this professional courtesy. She knows the Dragonfruit's rules and follows them, which is why she has been using the same corner booth for six years.

Her connection to both [[Gorduin Wynlar]] and [[Ayre Waesphyra]] began as a professional relationship (she conducted the Oracles tower's compliance assessment in Year 312) and evolved into something more useful for all parties. She is the only person Gorduin has given a personal contact sigil to outside the Oracles.
