# Dhymma Dherignol

[[Bonehands]] mid-level coordinator responsible for logistics and timing across [[Amberburgh]] district operations. She does not do fieldwork — her value is in the scheduling, the resource allocation, and the careful management of who knows what about whom. She has been in this role for eleven years, which is longer than most people in similar positions survive, which she attributes to consistent professionalism and an excellent memory for favors.

Dhymma looks like an accountant and works like one. She maintains three separate ledger systems — one for actual operations, one for the version she would show the [[Solarguard]] if raided, and one for her own records of who has lied to her. The third is the longest. She wears reading spectacles she does not need because they give her an excuse to look over them at people, which she finds useful in meetings.

Her longevity in the Bonehands is not accidental. She survived the internal purge seven years ago by being the only person who knew where all the supply caches were stored, and she has maintained that monopoly on logistical knowledge ever since. She considers this life insurance rather than leverage, though the distinction is academic.

She has a complicated respect for [[Zoughat]] — he is the only field operative who has never once been late to a scheduled position, and she rewards reliability with the closest thing she has to trust.
