# Grand Resonance Observatory

The Academy's primary arcane research facility, occupying the upper floors of the main Academy tower. Currently focused on monitoring the newly discovered resonance grid fragment. Closed to non-researchers. Access requires Archmage authorization or an extremely good reason.

The Observatory occupies the upper floors of the main [[Duskfrost Academy]] tower.
