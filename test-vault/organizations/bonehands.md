# Bonehands

The Bonehands control organized crime in the harbor and marsh districts of Duskfrost. They collect tribute, mediate disputes, and run the largest smuggling operation in the city. Their relationship with the [[Solarguard]] is an open secret — officially hostile, practically a cold war with mutually understood boundaries.
