# Kabugbu

An orc who operates as an intelligence asset within the [[Bonehands]] organization — someone who provides information to outside parties at significant personal risk. He is hard to reach, rarely available, and cannot be contacted consecutively without risking his cover.

Kabugbu's gift is ordinariness. In an organization full of memorable personalities — enforcers with reputations, coordinators with sharp minds, lookouts with visible discipline — he is the orc nobody thinks about twice. He runs errands, carries messages, shows up when told and leaves when dismissed. He has cultivated this invisibility for four years, and it costs him more than anyone will ever know.

The information he passes goes to [[Lieutenant Orvaine Shel]]'s intelligence network, though he has never met her directly. There are two cutouts between them. He does not know who runs the operation and does not want to. What he knows is that people in [[Bayside Shushail]] are getting hurt by Bonehands expansion, and he decided — in a moment of clarity he sometimes regrets — that he could do something about it without anyone noticing.

He is terrified most of the time. He manages this by being extremely good at his cover job, which means he is genuinely useful to the Bonehands, which means betraying them feels worse with every passing month. He has not slept well in two years.
