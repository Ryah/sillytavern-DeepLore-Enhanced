# Night of Empty Chairs

A coordinated assassination of three mid-tier Triumvirate functionaries in Year 178. The perpetrators were never identified. The event accelerated development of the [[Wardenship]]'s protective detail protocols.

Historians cite the [[War of Glimmering Hope]] as context for this event.
