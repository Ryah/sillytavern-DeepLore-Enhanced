# Great Census of Year 180

The first systematic census of Duskfrost, ordered by the Triumvirate in Year 180. It counted approximately 52,000 residents across all districts. The [[Highwallow]] count was disputed immediately, as census takers refused to enter several blocks. The official figure for Highwallow is generally considered an undercount.

Census records have become politically relevant again during the [[Velkhast Succession Crisis]], as claimants cite population figures to legitimize district-level support.
