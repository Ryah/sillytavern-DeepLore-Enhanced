# Adelas Gilydark

Half-elf junior researcher at the [[Oracles of Might]], assigned to [[Gorduin Wynlar]]'s cataloguing team. She has been in the position for three years and has developed a working theory that the cataloguing system exists in Gorduin's memory precisely so that no one can function without him. She has not voiced this theory to him.

Adelas is the sort of researcher who forgets meals when she finds something interesting, which happens approximately four times a week. She has a sharp analytical mind buried under three layers of institutional deference — she has learned that being right too quickly makes senior researchers uncomfortable, so she presents her conclusions as questions and lets them arrive at the same answer independently. This strategy is effective and slowly destroying her patience.

What Gorduin does not know — what no one at the Oracles knows — is that Adelas has been independently cross-referencing the cataloguing gaps she has identified with public records from the [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]]'s cultural archives. She has found seven entries that should exist in the Oracles' collection but do not, and the pattern of their absence suggests they were removed deliberately rather than lost. She is not yet sure what to do with this information, because the most likely person to have removed them is Gorduin himself.

She has a tentative friendship with [[Sarros Sylren]], who is the only other junior-level researcher willing to discuss uncomfortable hypotheses over lunch.
