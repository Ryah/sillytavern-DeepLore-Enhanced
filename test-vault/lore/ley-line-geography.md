# Ley Line Geography of Duskfrost

Three major ley lines converge beneath what is now the [[Highwallow]] district — this convergence is almost certainly why the pre-Calamity civilization built their primary resonance tower node there, and almost certainly why the Calamity did its most severe damage to that location. The Resonance Scar marks the intersection point.
