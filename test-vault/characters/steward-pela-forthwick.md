# Steward Pela Forthwick

Steward of the Triumvirate administrative compound, responsible for all domestic operations — staff, supply, maintenance, and the thousand daily decisions that keep a functioning seat of government operational. She has clearance to all non-restricted areas of the compound by virtue of her role, and encounters restricted information on a regular basis by virtue of being the person who organizes the rooms where it is discussed. She considers discretion a professional obligation rather than a personal choice.

The [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] considers her indispensable, which she finds professionally reassuring and personally exhausting.
