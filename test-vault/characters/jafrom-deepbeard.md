# Jafrom Deepbeard

Dwarf master stonemason who has held the maintenance contract for the [[Bellsummit]] tower complex for twenty-two years. He knows which walls are load-bearing, which are decorative, and which ones the Oracles have asked him not to look at directly. He honors all three categories with equal professionalism. His beard reaches his belt and is legendary in the local craftworkers' lodge.
