# Duskfrost Climate

Duskfrost has a temperate maritime climate dominated by the cold northern current and the warm harbor basin. The result is a city that is damp for most of the year, cold for half of it, and genuinely pleasant for approximately six weeks in late summer — a period the residents refer to as "the apology."

**Spring (Thaw — roughly 8 weeks):** The harbor ice breaks in early spring, reopening sea trade routes that have been closed for two to three months. Flooding in [[Highwallow]] peaks during snowmelt. The [[Solarguard]] doubles marsh district patrols during flood season, though this is more about preventing looting than providing aid.

**Summer (roughly 10 weeks):** Warm, humid, and the only season when the sea fog reliably lifts before midday. The harbor trade peaks. Open-air markets in [[Amberburgh]] extend their hours. [[Bellsummit]]'s arcane lanterns are unnecessary but remain lit — the Academy considers them a display of institutional reliability rather than illumination.

**Autumn (Grey — roughly 10 weeks):** Sea fog returns and thickens. Rain becomes persistent. The harbor remains navigable but unpleasant. Temperature drops noticeably on the upper bluffs. The [[Rimeborough]] district becomes comfortable for [[Delivese]] residents and increasingly hostile to everyone else.

**Winter (roughly 12 weeks):** The harbor's inner basin freezes in most years, shutting down deep-water trade. Coastal winds drive ice and sleet across the upper bluffs. The [[Cliff Stairs]] become hazardous. [[Scorchhelm]]'s stone walls accumulate frost that the fortress staff no longer bother clearing. Snow accumulates in the lower districts where buildings block the wind.

The fog is Duskfrost's defining weather feature. Harbor fog rolls in most mornings year-round, thickest in autumn and winter. It muffles sound, reduces visibility, and creates conditions that the [[Bonehands]] consider operationally favorable and the Solarguard consider a persistent security problem. On the densest fog days, the upper districts of Bellsummit sit above the fog line, looking down at a city that has temporarily vanished.

The Resonance Scar in [[Highwallow]] creates localized weather anomalies — sudden temperature drops, unexplained static charges, and occasional rain that falls warm. The Academy monitors these effects. The [[Keepers of the Scar]] record them with considerably more reverence.
