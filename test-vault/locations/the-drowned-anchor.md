# The Drowned Anchor

A dockside tavern in northern [[Bayside Shushail]] that operates on the principle that sailors require inexpensive drinks and no questions. The Drowned Anchor delivers on both. Rumors, shipping schedules, and crew availability are freely traded here. The [[Bonehands]] leave it alone because it's useful; the [[Solarguard]] leaves it alone because it's easier.
