# Snugug

Orc enforcer for the [[Bonehands]] in [[Bayside Shushail]]. Large, reliable, and possessed of a subtlety that his size leads most people to underestimate. He is not the sort of person you encounter in passing — he becomes relevant when someone is persistently asking the wrong questions.

Snugug keeps his nails trimmed and his clothes clean, which unsettles people more than if he looked the part they expect. He moves through the Bayside docks with the careful awareness of someone who knows exactly how much space he displaces and has opinions about when to use that fact. He does not raise his voice. He does not need to. When he speaks, it is in complete sentences with deliberate pauses, and the pauses do most of the work.

What most people don't know — and what [[Dhymma Dherignol]] suspects but has never confirmed — is that Snugug sends money to a small school in the outer Shushail wards. Not a lot. Enough to keep a roof patched and a teacher fed. He has never visited, never claimed credit, and becomes noticeably less conversational if anyone mentions the district's children in his presence. The enforcer work pays for more than enforcement, and he is not entirely comfortable with that arithmetic.

His working relationship with [[Harvel Quince]] is efficient and uncomplicated: Quince handles the conversations, and Snugug handles the conversations that follow the conversations. They have worked together for six years and have never shared a meal.
