# Redpond Waterfront

The developed shoreline of the Redpond lake, where the Fishers Cooperative maintains its docks, sorting facilities, and the cooperative hall. The waterfront also serves as an informal gathering point for Redpond residents in good weather. The lake is small enough that you can see across it from any point on the shore.

Shares administrative ties with [[Highwallow]].
