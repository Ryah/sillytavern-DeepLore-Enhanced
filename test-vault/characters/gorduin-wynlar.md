# Gorduin Wynlar

Gorduin Wynlar is the Head Archivist of the [[Oracles of Might]] — the person responsible for cataloguing, protecting, and occasionally explaining to concerned visitors why certain things in the vault are doing what they're doing. He has held this position for thirty-one years, through two changes of Grand Oracle, one near-catastrophic vault breach, and the ongoing situation with the [[Angel Crown]].

Gorduin is methodical in a way that most people find reassuring until they discover what he is methodically protecting. His office in the [[Bellsummit]] tower complex is immaculately organized, with a cataloguing system that exists entirely in his memory (he considers written indices a security risk). His personal library contains seventeen volumes that he has classified as too dangerous for the main vault and has therefore kept where he can watch them personally.

His relationship with the Angel Crown is the defining professional relationship of his career — it has been studying him as long as he has been studying it, and he suspects it has reached more conclusions.

He works closely with [[Ayre Waesphyra]] on Antecedent Culture research and maintains a formal, productive, and mutually suspicious relationship with [[Tybellan Hercyne]], whose field work has a habit of producing materials that should not exist. He considers [[Felicitas Langguth]] the only person in Duskfrost who reliably gets information to him before it becomes a crisis, which makes her useful.

His connection to [[Scorchhelm]] is indirect: the [[Compact Vault]] there stores documents he has studied extensively and would prefer to have copies of. The [[Solarguard]] has declined to provide copies. This is an ongoing negotiation.
