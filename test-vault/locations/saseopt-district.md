# Saseopt District

Saseopt is a mixed district — residential near the center, light commercial on its edges, and increasingly gentrifying as [[Amberburgh]] expands. It hosts the [[Cirilae]] cultural hall and several mid-tier guild offices that couldn't afford Amberburgh rents.
