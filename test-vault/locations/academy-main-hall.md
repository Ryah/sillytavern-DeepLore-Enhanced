# Academy Main Hall

The [[Duskfrost Academy]]'s primary assembly and lecture space — a high-ceilinged hall seating approximately 400, used for all-faculty meetings, public lectures, annual advancement ceremonies, and the occasional Triumvirate-adjacent reception. The acoustic resonance stones in the ceiling have not required recalibration in eleven years, which the Academy's facilities staff considers a personal achievement.
