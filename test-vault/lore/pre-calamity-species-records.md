# Pre-Calamity Species Records

The Pallasys Translations suggest the Antecedent Culture was multispecies, with at least six distinct populations represented in the resonance tower administrative records. Which of these correspond to any currently existing species is contested — the translation accuracy for species-descriptive terms is among the lowest in the corpus, estimated at 35%.

The [[Duskfrost Academy]] natural history department is the primary institution studying these records.
