# Solarguard Headquarters

The Solarguard's administrative core sits at the center of the [[Scorchhelm]] fortress complex — command offices, holding cells, records vault, and the watch commander's suite. All district patrol officers report here for shift changes. Officially open to the public for complaints and missing person reports. In practice, the public finds reasons to avoid it.
