# Zoughat

Orc lookout stationed on the [[Amberburgh]] dockside for the [[Bonehands]]. He does not speak during operations unless spoken to, which suits everyone.

<div class="meta-block">

</div>

Zoughat is unnervingly still. Where other lookouts fidget, pace, or find ways to pass time, he stands in his assigned position like architecture. Dock workers who pass him daily have reported forgetting he was there, which is the entire point. He sees everything on the waterfront — who arrives, who lingers, who looks at something they shouldn't — and communicates this to [[Dhymma Dherignol]] through a system of dead drops rather than conversation.

The silence is not shyness. Zoughat grew up in the outer camps where talking drew attention and attention drew violence, and he learned early that the quietest person in a room is usually the last one standing. He has carried this principle into professional life with complete commitment. He has opinions — about the Bonehands' operational security, about [[Snugug]]'s approach to enforcement, about the dockside customs officers — but sharing them would require speaking, which he considers an unnecessary risk.

He keeps a small journal in orcish shorthand. Nobody in the Bonehands knows about it. The contents would be extremely interesting to [[Agent Corvin Mast]] if they ever surfaced.
