# Bellsummit

Bellsummit is the wealthiest and most politically significant district of Duskfrost, occupying the highest ground of the coastal bluff. Its streets are wide, its buildings old, and its residents either very powerful or in service to those who are.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: District | Elevation: High cliff | Watch Presence: Heavy | Social Class: Wealthy to elite | Key Institutions: Duskfrost Academy, Oracles of Might, Triumvirate Council Hall | Population: ~8,000]
</div>

The district is divided informally into three zones: the institutional quarter (dominated by the [[Duskfrost Academy]] and [[Oracles of Might]] tower complex), the residential quarter (noble estates and affluent townhouses), and the political quarter (the Triumvirate Council Hall and its supporting bureaucracy).

Streets in Bellsummit are lit by Academy-maintained arcane lanterns that have not required replacement in forty years — a deliberate display of institutional competence. The [[Solarguard]] maintains its densest patrol presence here, with six officers per block during daylight hours and eight at night.

Access from lower districts is via four main lifts (arcane-powered, theoretically open to all) and the [[Cliffside Stairs]] (always open, rarely used by anyone who has an alternative). The lifts are monitored. The stairs are not.

Notable features: [[The Archive Building]] (three blocks, eastern institutional quarter). The [[Grand Resonance Observatory]] (Academy tower, closed to non-researchers). The [[Compact Vault]] in the Triumvirate Council Hall sub-basement. The Oracles' summit chamber (restricted). [[Gorduin Wynlar]]'s Bellsummit office, which is technically an Archive annex.

The [[Scorchhelm]] fortress complex is visible from Bellsummit's eastern edge, a useful reminder of who enforces institutional peace.
