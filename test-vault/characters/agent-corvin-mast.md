# Agent Corvin Mast

Triumvirate intelligence agent specializing in domestic threat assessment — monitoring internal political movements, identifying individuals who represent risk to state stability, and managing the information this generates. He operates under administrative cover as an "auditor," which is technically accurate in that he audits people's activities and associations rather than their finances. He has been watching the [[Bonehands]] situation in [[Bayside Shushail]] with increasing professional interest.

Corvin's greatest professional asset is that he looks like nobody. Not ugly, not handsome, not tall, not short — the kind of face that witnesses describe as "average build, brown hair, I think" and then cannot elaborate further. He has cultivated this natural advantage into a discipline. He owns clothes appropriate to every district and social stratum in Duskfrost and can change his apparent class in a public latrine in under three minutes.

His interest in the Bonehands is not idle. He has been assembling a case file for fourteen months that maps their expansion pattern, and the pattern does not look like organic criminal growth — it looks like someone is funding a rapid capability buildup for a specific purpose he cannot yet identify. He has requested additional resources from the Triumvirate twice and been denied both times, which he interprets as either bureaucratic inertia or active interference. He is not yet sure which, and the distinction matters.

He is aware of [[Lieutenant Orvaine Shel]]'s parallel intelligence operation at Scorchhelm but has not made contact, because Triumvirate domestic intelligence and Solarguard military intelligence do not coordinate formally. This is, in his professional opinion, a structural failure that will eventually produce a catastrophe. He has written a memo to this effect. It was filed.
