# Siege of Am — Background Lore

The Siege of Am was not primarily a military action — it was a negotiation by other means. The proto-Triumvirate needed Am's harbor access. Am's leaders knew this. The siege was how the Triumvirate demonstrated they were serious. The failure was deliberate — a face-saving retreat that let Am accept the subsequent trade treaty without appearing conquered.

Understanding requires familiarity with the [[War of Glimmering Hope]].
