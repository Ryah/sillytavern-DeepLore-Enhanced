# Zuni Dhegevnac

Zuni Dhegevnac sells information. This is the polite description. The complete description is that Zuni is Duskfrost's most reliable private intelligence resource — the person you approach when official channels have failed, when you cannot ask publicly, or when you need information that technically does not exist.

Zuni does not work for anyone. She works with people, and the distinction is enforced by a carefully maintained web of mutual useful relationships that would collapse spectacularly if she were ever perceived as affiliated rather than independent. The Bonehands know things she needs. The Oracles need things the Bonehands know. She is the efficient middle.

Her professional relationship with [[Gorduin Wynlar]] is the most productive of these arrangements. He provides her with occasional academic identification for accessing restricted libraries. She provides him with information about what is moving through the black market that might be of arcane concern. Neither acknowledges this arrangement in writing.

Her history with [[Grolgrurim Bluntgut]] is not something either of them discusses publicly. They do not appear in the same rooms when it can be avoided. The reason involves a contract, a dwarf trading consortium, and approximately forty thousand gold crowns in missing funds that Grolgrurim believes were stolen and Zuni believes were legitimately recovered. These positions are irreconcilable.
