# Lorant

The Lorant are a winged humanoid species nesting on the cliff faces above [[Bellsummit]]. Territorial and proud. They serve as licensed aerial messengers between the cliff districts and have a long-standing treaty with the Triumvirate granting them rights to the upper cliff face.
