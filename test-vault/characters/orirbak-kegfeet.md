# Orirbak Kegfeet

Dwarf master brewer and serving treasurer for the [[Marblesmiths]] guild in [[Amberburgh]]. The brewing and the treasury are not unrelated — he finances the guild's social functions through his brewery's surplus, which gives him a structural relationship with everyone who attends guild events. He has never used this leverage overtly. He does not need to.

Orirbak is the sort of dwarf who makes humans comfortable with dwarves — loud, generous, always ready with a tankard and a story. This is genuine. It is also strategy. A treasurer who is liked gets less scrutiny than one who is feared, and Orirbak has made himself so central to the guild's social life that questioning his accounts would feel like questioning a friend. His accounts are, in fact, impeccable. He simply prefers that nobody check.

What the guild does not know is that Orirbak has been quietly diverting a fraction of the brewery surplus into a private reserve fund — not for personal enrichment, but because he has been watching the Bonehands' expansion into Amberburgh's trade channels and believes the Marblesmiths will need emergency capital within the next two years. He has not discussed this with [[Grolgrurim Bluntgut]] because Grolgrurim would want to fight the problem head-on, and Orirbak believes some problems are better survived than confronted.

He considers Grolgrurim the guild's sword and himself its shield, and he has arranged the treasury accordingly. They have been partners in guild leadership for thirty years and disagree about almost everything except the fundamental principle that the Marblesmiths endure.
