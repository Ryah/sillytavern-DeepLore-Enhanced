# Bayside Shushail

Bayside Shushail is where ships dock, fish is unloaded, smuggled goods change hands, and the [[Bonehands]] conduct most of their visible business. It smells of salt, tar, fish, and sometimes things that don't have polite names. It is loud during the day and a different kind of loud at night.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: District | Elevation: Harbor level | Watch Presence: Nominal | Social Class: Working to poor | Key Organizations: Bonehands, [[Salvagers Compact]], [[Old Harbor Brotherhood]] | Population: ~22,000]
</div>

The district is divided between the working harbor (north — docks, warehouses, legitimate trade) and the settlement quarter (south — dense residential, markets, taverns). The Bonehands maintain a public "office" — technically a counting house — on the primary dock street that everyone knows is their administrative headquarters.

The [[Solarguard]]'s presence is a formality. The six officers assigned to Bayside Shushail know who actually controls the district and have adjusted their behavior accordingly. Serious crimes that cross the Bonehands' interests are handled by the Bonehands directly. Everything else is handled by the Solarguard or ignored.

[[Zuvine]] and [[Bhissalae]] communities are concentrated in the northern quarter near the water access points. Relations between the two species are professionally managed and personally tense.

[[The Hidden Dragonfruit Tavern]] is in the mid-district settlement quarter, deliberately unremarkable from outside.
