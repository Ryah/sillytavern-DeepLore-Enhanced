# Kethvali

Kethvali are nomadic traders who pass through Duskfrost twice yearly with caravans from distant regions. They do not settle. Their goods — spices, textiles, preserved foods, and occasional arcane curiosities — command premium prices.

Their primary trade route passes through [[Scorchhelm]] before reaching the interior markets.
