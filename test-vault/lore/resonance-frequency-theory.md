# Resonance Frequency Theory

The current Academy model holds that arcane energy operates in discrete frequency bands, similar to sound harmonics. The pre-Calamity resonance network achieved its extraordinary power by aligning the frequencies of all towers simultaneously — essentially creating a city-wide chord. The current research question is whether the discovered fragment is still broadcasting its frequency, and whether anything nearby is listening.

Understanding requires familiarity with the [[War of Glimmering Hope]].
