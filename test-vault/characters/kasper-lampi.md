# Kasper Lampi

[[Solarguard]] sergeant at [[Scorchhelm]], assigned to the outer perimeter defense rotation. He has been in the Guard for fourteen years and holds no illusions about promotion — he is a sergeant because he is exceptionally good at being a sergeant, and he intends to remain one until retirement. His unit's casualty rate is the lowest in the current rotation, which he attributes to preparation and an aggressive policy on not taking unnecessary risks.

Kasper looks like what he is: a man who has spent fourteen years sleeping on cold ground and eating field rations and has made peace with both. His armor is never polished but always functional. He mends his own kit and insists his soldiers do the same, on the theory that a man who can't fix his own gear can't be trusted to fix anything else. He is universally liked by his unit and universally avoided by officers who mistake his bluntness for insubordination.

The thing Kasper doesn't talk about is that he was offered a commission twice — once by [[Commander Bryndas Holte]] directly — and turned it down both times. Officers make decisions that get people killed. Sergeants keep people alive. He decided a long time ago which job he wanted, and the decision costs him nothing except a larger pension, which he considers a fair trade.

He has a quiet respect for [[Paladin Serevath Dul]], whom he encountered once during a joint patrol. She waited three hours for the right moment to engage a threat his unit had been harassing ineffectively. He's been thinking about that ever since.
