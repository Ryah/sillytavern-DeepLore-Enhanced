# Oracles Schism

In Year 271, a faction of the [[Oracles of Might]] split over the question of publication — specifically, whether dangerous research should be shared with the broader scholarly community or kept internal. The faction that favored open publication left to found the [[Mages of the Patient]]. Relations between the two organizations have been chilly ever since.
