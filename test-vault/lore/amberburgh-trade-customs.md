# Amberburgh Trade Customs

Amberburgh trade runs on three parallel systems: the official Triumvirate-regulated market, the guild-mediated secondary market, and the night market. The customs governing each are distinct and occasionally contradictory. The one universal rule: a witnessed handshake in Amberburgh is legally binding regardless of whether any paperwork follows. This custom predates the Triumvirate and has survived every attempt to replace it with something more formal.
