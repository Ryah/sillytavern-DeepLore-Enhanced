# Agent Corvin Mast

Triumvirate intelligence agent specializing in domestic threat assessment — monitoring internal political movements, identifying individuals who represent risk to state stability, and managing the information this generates. He operates under administrative cover as an "auditor," which is technically accurate in that he audits people's activities and associations rather than their finances. He has been watching the [[Bonehands]] situation in [[Bayside Shushail]] with increasing professional interest.

Has had several notable encounters with [[Kabugbu]].
