# Scorchhelm Fortifications

The Scorchhelm fortifications were built in three distinct phases: the original Reconstruction-era earthworks (Year 40-80), the stone curtain wall added during the First Consolidation War (Year 130), and the arcane ward network added following the [[War of the Broken Mountain]] (Year 290). Each layer reflects the threat model of its era. The Reconstruction earthworks, preserved inside the later walls, are still considered structurally sound by engineers who have examined them and structurally baffling by engineers who understand the materials involved.
