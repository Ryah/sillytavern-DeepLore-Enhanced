# Scar Mapping Expedition

A joint Academy-[[Solarguard]] expedition in Year 198 attempted to map the full extent of the Resonance Scar in [[Highwallow]]. The expedition mapped approximately 70% of the Scar boundary before equipment failures (attributed to Scar interference) forced withdrawal. The remaining 30% is still not officially mapped.
