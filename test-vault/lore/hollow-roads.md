# The Hollow Roads

The Hollow Roads are a network of pre-Calamity tunnels beneath and around Duskfrost, connecting to an unknown number of other locations at unknown distances. The Academy has mapped approximately 20% of the accessible tunnel network. The [[Gnaino]] know more. They are not sharing.
