# Ye Olde Guinea Pig Inn

A well-regarded inn in East Hamp with clean rooms, reasonable prices, and an unexplained guinea pig motif throughout its decor. Popular with [[Marblesmiths]] guild members and traveling tradespeople. Does not actually serve guinea pig.
