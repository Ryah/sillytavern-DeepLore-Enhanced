# Deep Harbor Ecology

The [[Bayside Shushail]] harbor supports an unusual mix of species concentrated near the thermal vent field at its center. Several species found here exist nowhere else in the known coast — possibly descended from populations that survived the Calamity in the thermal plumes. Fishers report that the deep-water catch changes character approximately every seven years in ways that correlate, loosely, with the Resonance Scar's measurement cycles.

Understanding requires familiarity with the [[Thornwick Pallasys Biography]].
