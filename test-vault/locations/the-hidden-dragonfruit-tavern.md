# The Hidden Dragonfruit Tavern

The Hidden Dragonfruit Tavern is not hidden and does not serve dragonfruit. It is, however, one of the most important establishments in [[Bayside Shushail]] — not for its food (adequate) or its drink (serviceable) but for the informal neutrality it has maintained for forty years.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: Tavern | Location: Bayside Shushail, mid-district | Owner: Kellis Dorn | Notable Patrons: [[Felicitas Langguth]], various | Reputation: Neutral ground | Capacity: ~60]
</div>

Owner Kellis Dorn established the tavern's neutrality by a simple method: he refuses to work with anyone exclusively, which means he works with everyone. [[Bonehands]] use a back room. [[Solarguard]] officers drink at the bar without armor, by mutual agreement. Merchants League representatives hold informal lunches on Tuesdays. The [[Oracles of Might]] once held a recruitment meeting in the cellar and paid generously for the inconvenience.

The neutrality is maintained by three rules: no weapons drawn on the premises, no business discussed at the bar (private rooms available), and no one followed when they leave. The rules have been violated twice in forty years. Both violators left Bayside Shushail the following morning and were encouraged not to return.

Felicitas Langguth is the tavern's most regular patron — she holds a standing reservation of the corner booth on Mondays and Thursdays. Most of what she knows about the harbor district she learned here.
