# Cecilie Sorensen

Human physician who runs the [[Bayside Shushail]] public clinic — the only medical facility in the district that does not require a guild card or Triumvirate registration. As a result, she treats [[Solarguard]] soldiers, [[Bonehands]] members, and ordinary citizens without distinction, which makes her simultaneously useful to everyone and trusted by no one fully. She finds this arrangement professionally sustainable if personally exhausting.

Cecilie's clinic is two rooms above a chandler's shop — one for examinations, one for recovery. She has four beds and treats an average of thirty patients a day, which means the beds are for people who literally cannot stand. She buys supplies at cost from contacts she's cultivated over twenty years of practice and performs surgery by lantern light when necessary, which is more often than it should be. She does not complain about the conditions because complaining takes time she does not have.

The neutrality she maintains between factions is genuine but not complete. She keeps records — not medical records, which she guards fiercely under physician's oath — but a separate tally of injuries by type and district origin. She has noticed patterns: the same kinds of wounds appearing in clusters, correlating with events she reads about days later. She has shared this information exactly once, with [[Brother Emvel Crayne]], who understood immediately what it meant and asked her to keep counting.

What nobody in Bayside Shushail knows is that Cecilie trained at the [[Bellsummit]] Academy's medical faculty and left under circumstances that resulted in her license being restricted to "community practice" — a polite term for exile from institutional medicine. The reason involves a patient, a senior faculty member, and a choice she would make again without hesitation. [[Hedge Mage Pruvven Alt]] refers cases to her that exceed his competence; she refers cases to him that require magic she cannot provide. Neither has ever discussed the arrangement formally.
