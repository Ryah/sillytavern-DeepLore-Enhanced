# Selaveth

Selaveth are rare — fewer than forty in all of Duskfrost. They perceive arcane energy fields directly, without instruments. This makes them invaluable to the Academy, which employs all it can find at above-market rates.

[[Ayre Waesphyra]] has published the only peer-reviewed study of Selaveth arcane perception.
