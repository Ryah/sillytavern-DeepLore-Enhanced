# War of the Broken Mountain

The War of the Broken Mountain (Year 289-293) was a four-year armed conflict triggered by House Harcyne's attempt to seize direct administrative control of the [[Duskfrost Academy]] following the death of Archmage Pallasys IV.

The war began as a legal dispute. House Harcyne argued that the Archmage's death without a designated successor created a vacancy in the Founding Charter that granted the Triumvirate provisional control. The Academy disputed this interpretation forcefully, and when Harcyne forces attempted to occupy the Academy's administrative tower, the [[Wardenship]] responded with lethal force.

The following four years saw a low-intensity urban conflict fought primarily through proxy forces — Harcyne-aligned mercenaries against Academy Wardens and their allies in the [[Champions of Patience]]. The [[Solarguard]] maintained formal neutrality while selectively enforcing laws that disadvantaged whichever side was currently winning.

The conflict ended with the [[Bellsummit]] Accord, a renegotiated version of the Founding Charter that further restricted Triumvirate influence over Academy governance while granting Harcyne significant trade concessions in the harbor. Both sides declared victory. Neither was correct.

The war left lasting damage to the Bellsummit district — several blocks of the upper hill still show scorch marks from Wardenship defensive fires. More significantly, it established the precedent that the Academy would use force to defend its institutional independence, a fact every subsequent Triumvirate has had to account for.

Some historians believe the [[Academy Fire of Year 211]] destroyed records that would have changed the outcome of negotiations.
