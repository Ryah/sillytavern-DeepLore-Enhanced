# Oracles of Might Tower

The Oracles of Might occupy a six-story tower complex on the western edge of [[Bellsummit]]'s institutional quarter, adjacent to the [[Duskfrost Academy]] but emphatically separate from it. The building predates the Academy by at least a century — the stonework on the lower floors is rougher, darker, laid in a style no living mason can identify. The upper floors contain the guild's private arcane collection, larger than the [[The Archive Building|Archive]]'s restricted section according to persistent rumor. Access above the third floor requires full guild membership. The summit chamber is restricted to the Oracles' inner council, and the staircase leading to it has been observed to rearrange itself when unauthorized visitors attempt the climb.

The ground floor functions as the guild's public face: a reception hall of polished basalt, perpetually lit by cold blue luminescence that casts no shadows. The air tastes faintly of ozone and old copper. Petitioners wait on stone benches worn smooth by generations of nervous sitting. The second and third floors house offices, meeting chambers, and the guild's administrative records — contracts, dispute arbitrations, and the careful accounting of favors owed and favors spent. The smell of candle wax and sealing resin is thick enough to coat the tongue. Guild clerks move through these corridors with the quiet urgency of people who understand that arcane governance generates more paperwork than arcane research.

Above the third floor, the tower changes character. The stone grows colder. Sound behaves differently — footsteps echo before they land, and conversations in adjacent rooms arrive as unintelligible whispers through walls that should be too thick to transmit anything. The private collection occupies floors four and five, its contents catalogued by a system the [[Oracles of Might]] have never shared with the Academy. Visiting researchers from the Archive have described the experience of browsing these shelves as "deeply unsettling in a way that had nothing to do with the content of the books." The sixth floor summit chamber is where the inner council meets, and the tower's roof serves as the guild's primary observation platform — the highest point in Bellsummit, offering an unobstructed view of the entire city and the sea beyond.

The [[Solarguard]] maintains a small watch post at the tower's base, officially for security purposes. In practice, they note who enters and when, information that finds its way to the [[Scorchhelm]] records vault with reliable frequency. The Oracles are aware of this. They consider it beneath comment.
