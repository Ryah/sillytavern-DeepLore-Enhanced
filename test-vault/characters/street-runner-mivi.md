# Mivi

Street runner operating across all Duskfrost districts — she carries messages between parties who prefer not to use official channels, delivers packages that official couriers won't ask questions about, and moves faster through the city's informal geography than most people move through the streets. She is seventeen years old, knows every district by feel rather than map, and has a strict professional policy of not reading messages or asking what is in packages. She has been offered better-paying work twice and declined both times on the grounds that she prefers her current freedom of movement.

Mivi knows Duskfrost the way most people know their own home — by the shortcuts, the sounds, the way the air changes between districts. She can cross from Bayside Shushail to Bellsummit in fourteen minutes using a route that goes through two basements, across a rooftop, and along a drainage channel that the city planners forgot about. She has mapped these routes in her head and nowhere else, because writing them down would make her replaceable.

She does not read the messages she carries. This is not honor — it is insurance. A runner who knows nothing cannot betray anything, and a runner who cannot betray anything stays alive. She learned this principle from a runner who preceded her and who is no longer alive, which she considers sufficient validation of the policy.

She has worked alongside [[Ayre Waesphyra]] on multiple occasions and considers her the most competent adult she has met, which is a high compliment from someone who trusts almost nobody. She brings coffee and district news to [[Corporal Thresh Dunmore]] at the eastern gate, an arrangement that keeps her passage smooth and gives her a contact inside the [[Solarguard]] she can use if she ever needs one. She tells herself she won't need one. She keeps the arrangement anyway.
