# Arcane Bloodline Inheritance

Arcane affinity does not follow simple inheritance rules. Strong affinity in both parents does not guarantee affinity in children; two non-affinity parents occasionally produce a child with exceptional talent. The Academy's current model treats arcane affinity as polygenic with significant environmental modulation — a description that explains the data without explaining anything useful about prediction.

The [[Duskfrost Academy]] maintains the most comprehensive genealogical records tracking arcane affinity across generations.
