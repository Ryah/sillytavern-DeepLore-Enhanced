# Master Aldric Voss

Master of the [[Amberburgh]] Merchant Consortium — the coordinating body above individual guilds that manages inter-guild disputes, sets common trade standards, and represents commercial interests to the Triumvirate administration. He has held this role for twelve years through the force of his relationships, his memory for favors, and his willingness to absorb short-term losses for long-term positioning. He and [[Grolgrurim Bluntgut]] have a productive working relationship that neither would describe as friendly.
