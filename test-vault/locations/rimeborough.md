# Rimeborough

A northern clifftop district kept cold by persistent sea winds. The [[Delivese]] community has settled here in significant numbers, finding the temperature comfortable. Other species find it unpleasant. The district is quiet, clean, and largely self-sufficient.

Trade routes connect this area to [[Bellsummit]].
