# Vorruk

Vorruk are large (averaging 6'8", heavily muscular) humanoids with thick grey skin. They dominate the physical labor sector in [[Scorchhelm]] and the docks. Their size and strength make them natural choices for security work, a fact the [[Bonehands]] have thoroughly exploited.
