# Academy Main Hall

The [[Duskfrost Academy]]'s primary assembly and lecture space — a high-ceilinged hall seating approximately 400, used for all-faculty meetings, public lectures, annual advancement ceremonies, and the occasional Triumvirate-adjacent reception. The acoustic resonance stones in the ceiling have not required recalibration in eleven years, which the Academy's facilities staff considers a personal achievement.

The hall occupies the ground floor of the Academy's central wing in [[Bellsummit]], accessible through double doors of dark oak reinforced with iron banding that has been polished by centuries of hands pushing through. Inside, the space opens upward — three stories of vaulted stone ceiling supported by columns carved with the names of every Archmage who has held the position. The most recent additions are neat and precise. The oldest are nearly illegible, worn smooth by students who touch them for luck before examinations. Tiered stone seating rises on three sides, the wood bench-tops replaced every few decades but the stone foundations unchanged since construction. The fourth wall holds the speaker's platform, a raised stone dais where lecturers stand beneath a particularly dense cluster of resonance stones that amplify speech to every corner of the hall without distortion.

During term, the hall is never truly empty. Morning lectures draw rows of students in various states of attentiveness — the front rows alert, the back rows negotiating consciousness, the middle rows pretending to take notes. The smell is chalk dust, old stone, and the faint residual tang of whatever arcane demonstration was last performed on the dais. Between lectures, students cluster in the aisles arguing about theory, and the sound of four hundred overlapping conversations generates a hum that can be heard from the corridor outside. During advancement ceremonies, the hall fills to standing capacity, the resonance stones carrying the names of advancing students to the upper galleries where families crowd against the railings.

The hall also serves as the Academy's de facto political stage. When the Triumvirate needs to address [[Bellsummit]]'s institutional community, they do it here, standing on the same dais where first-year students deliver their qualifying presentations. The [[Oracles of Might]] consider this arrangement a deliberate power play. The Academy insists it is simply the largest indoor space available. Both statements are true.
