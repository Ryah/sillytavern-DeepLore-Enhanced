# Oracles of Might

The Oracles of Might is Duskfrost's oldest and most prestigious arcane research guild, founded in the early Reconstruction era by scholars who disagreed with the Academy's increasingly conservative approach to pre-Calamity knowledge. Where the Academy catalogued and restricted, the Oracles studied and applied.

<div class="meta-block">
[Type: Mage Guild | Founded: Year 89 | Location: [[Bellsummit]] | Head: Grand Oracle Tessavel Orindal (also Archmage — a deliberate power consolidation) | Members: ~120 full, ~300 associate | Alignment: Nominally neutral, practically pro-Morveth]
</div>

The guild occupies a tower complex adjacent to the Academy — close enough to share resources, separate enough to maintain institutional independence. Its library rivals the Archive's restricted section for depth if not breadth.

The Oracles operate under the principle of "informed risk" — dangerous knowledge should be studied by those equipped to study it rather than locked away where it cannot be contextualized or countered. This philosophy has produced both the guild's greatest discoveries and its most catastrophic accidents.

**Current political position:** The Oracles are quietly aligned with House Morveth in the current succession crisis, though this alignment is deniable. They are officially neutral.

**Relationship with the Academy:** Complex. The current Archmage (Tessavel Orindal) is simultaneously the Grand Oracle, which makes the institutional relationship deliberately tangled. Neither body will confirm whether this dual role represents alignment or leverage.

**Relationship with the [[Bonehands]]:** The Oracles maintain discreet channels with artifact dealers in [[Bayside Shushail]], purchasing pre-Calamity items that surface through the harbor's grey market. This practice is technically legal — the Academy's acquisition charter does not extend to the Oracles — but it places them in occasional contact with [[Bonehands]] intermediaries, a relationship neither party acknowledges publicly.

**Notable members:** [[Gorduin Wynlar]] (Head Archivist), [[Ayre Waesphyra]] (Senior Researcher, Antecedent Culture), [[Sarros Sylren]] (Field Researcher).

**Known projects:** Resonance grid mapping (classified), Pallasys Translation verification (ongoing), three unnamed projects at highest restriction level.

The Oracles maintain a tense working relationship with the [[Salvagers Compact]], whose field teams occasionally recover artifacts the Oracles consider theirs by institutional right.
