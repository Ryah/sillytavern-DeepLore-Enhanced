# Dockmaster Bram Solvik

Dockmaster of [[Bayside Shushail]]'s main cargo pier — the person who controls berth allocation, oversees cargo inspection, and certifies that manifests reflect the actual contents of holds. He is methodical, experienced, and has a strong professional relationship with both the [[Solarguard]] customs office and the [[Bonehands]] dockside operation. He considers this relationship management rather than corruption, and the distinction is fine enough that he is technically correct.
