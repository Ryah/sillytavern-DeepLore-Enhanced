# Tybellan Hercyne

Tybellan Hercyne has the distinction of being simultaneously a field researcher for the [[Oracles of Might]] and an affiliate of the [[Champions of Patience]] — two institutional identities that sit uneasily together and describe him fairly accurately. He is principled in the way that makes missions harder, and competent in the way that makes them succeed anyway.

Tybellan's field work produces a disproportionate share of the Oracles' most significant acquisitions, mostly because he goes to places that methodical researchers do not and returns from them, which cannot be taken for granted. He has a talent for detection magic that the Oracles find professionally useful and the Champions find morally reassuring.

His half-elven heritage makes him politically complicated in Bellsummit, where elf and human factions have historically had tense relationships. He manages this by being demonstrably useful to enough people that arguing about his parentage has a visible opportunity cost.

His in_chat injection at role:user represents his voice entering the conversation directly — he tends to appear when people are discussing what to do about a situation he has relevant field experience with.
