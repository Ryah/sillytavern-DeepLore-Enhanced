# Gnaino

The Gnaino are a subterranean species adapted to life in the deep tunnel networks beneath Duskfrost. They have poor surface vision but exceptional echolocation. Few are found above ground; those who are typically work as miners or tunnel surveyors.

They navigate the [[The Hollow Roads]] with an ease that surface dwellers find unsettling.
