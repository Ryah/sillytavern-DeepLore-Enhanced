# Salvagers Compact

The Salvagers Compact holds the city license for underwater salvage work in the harbor. Membership is majority [[Zuvine]] and [[Bhissalae]], with significant internal tension between the two species. The Academy buys everything the Compact brings up that it deems arcane.
