# Amberburgh Merchants League

The Amberburgh Merchants League is the trade association covering most commercial activity in the market district. It mediates disputes, lobbies the Triumvirate on tariff policy, and runs the licensed market stalls. Membership is mandatory for established traders; the fee is controversial.
