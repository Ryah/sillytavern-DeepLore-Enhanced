# Founding of the Solarguard

The Solarguard was formally established in Year 243 under the Watch Charter, a document negotiated over four years between all three Triumvirate houses and the Academy. Each faction wanted control of the city's law enforcement apparatus; the Watch Charter gave none of them direct control by creating a semi-independent command structure.

The compromise satisfied no one entirely and survived precisely because of that. The Solarguard's founding commander, Warden-General Elara Sundstep, spent her twenty-year tenure carefully avoiding obligation to any single faction, a tradition her successors have honored with varying degrees of success.

The Watch Charter's ambiguous language about [[Wardenship]] jurisdiction — the source of three centuries of institutional friction — was not an oversight. It was a deliberate compromise that each faction believed secretly favored its own interests. In this sense, the Watch Charter is a masterwork of political drafting.
