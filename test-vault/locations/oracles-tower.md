# Oracles of Might Tower

The Oracles of Might occupy a six-story tower complex on the western edge of [[Bellsummit]]'s institutional quarter, adjacent to the Academy but emphatically separate from it. The upper floors contain the guild's private arcane collection — larger than the Archive's restricted section, according to persistent rumor. Access above the third floor requires full guild membership. The summit chamber is restricted to the Oracles' inner council.
