# Siege of Am

A failed siege of an early Reconstruction-era settlement that Duskfrost attempted to absorb by force. The failure led to a negotiated trade treaty that eventually accomplished the same goal. Military historians cite it as an early example of Triumvirate preference for economic over military conquest.

Veterans of the siege eventually settled in [[Bellsummit]], where their descendants still maintain a minor veterans'  guild.
