# Zoughat

Orc lookout stationed on the [[Amberburgh]] dockside for the [[Bonehands]]. He does not speak during operations unless spoken to, which suits everyone. His excludeRecursion flag means he is relevant when you come to him directly, not when following a chain of links that happens to pass through his name.
