# Lieutenant Orvaine Shel

Half-elf [[Solarguard]] lieutenant serving as intelligence liaison to Commander Holte at [[Scorchhelm]]. Her role involves coordinating information from civilian sources, managing the Guard's informant network in the eastern districts, and translating what civilians report into operationally useful intelligence. She is careful, methodical, and acutely aware that her half-elven status means she has to be twice as visibly competent to be perceived as half as capable.

Orvaine uses three colors of ink: black for confirmed intelligence, blue for unverified reports, and red for connections she suspects but cannot prove. Her current notebook has more red than she is comfortable with. The Bonehands are expanding operations in ways that suggest coordination she cannot yet trace to a source, and [[Kabugbu]]'s reports — filtered through two cutouts — describe an organization that is becoming more disciplined rather than less. This concerns her professionally.

What concerns her personally is that she is beginning to suspect one of her other informants is feeding her deliberately shaped intelligence — not false, exactly, but curated in ways that guide her attention toward some threats and away from others. She has not reported this suspicion to [[Commander Bryndas Holte]] because she does not yet know who benefits from the shaping, and premature disclosure would burn the source before she can determine the answer.

She maintains a careful professional distance from everyone at Scorchhelm except Holte, who promoted her and whom she repays with absolute operational honesty. She has never told him that she applied to the Solarguard after being rejected from the [[Duskfrost Academy]] — twice — for reasons that were never formally stated and never needed to be.
