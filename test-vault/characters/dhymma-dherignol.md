# Dhymma Dherignol

[[Bonehands]] mid-level coordinator responsible for logistics and timing across [[Amberburgh]] district operations. She does not do fieldwork — her value is in the scheduling, the resource allocation, and the careful management of who knows what about whom. She has been in this role for eleven years, which is longer than most people in similar positions survive, which she attributes to consistent professionalism and an excellent memory for favors.

Has had several notable encounters with [[Scrivener Tulla Nance]].
