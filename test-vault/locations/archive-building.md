# The Archive Building

The Duskfrost Archive occupies three city blocks in [[Bellsummit]]'s institutional quarter — a chaotic collection of architectural styles representing seven construction phases. Public collection on the ground floor, restricted above, Black Shelf somewhere in the upper levels. Head Archivist Brimstone Kettleback has worked here for 31 years.
