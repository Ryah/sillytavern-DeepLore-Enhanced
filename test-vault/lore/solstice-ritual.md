# Solstice Alignment Ritual

The Solstice Alignment Ritual is an annual ceremony conducted by the [[Oracles of Might]] at winter solstice in the [[Bellsummit]] tower's summit chamber. The ritual has been performed continuously for over two centuries, with a brief interruption during the [[War of the Broken Mountain]].

The ritual's stated purpose is to recalibrate the tower's arcane instrumentation using the specific astronomical alignment that occurs at solstice. This is accurate as far as it goes. The ritual's secondary purpose — which [[Gorduin Wynlar]] would describe as the "actual purpose" — is to monitor the Resonance Scar's annual fluctuation cycle and compare it to historical records.

The Scar fluctuates predictably over the course of a year, with a specific pattern at winter solstice that the Oracles have been measuring for 200 years. The pattern has been changing, very slowly, for the past fifteen years. The change is outside normal variation. No one has published this finding.

The ritual requires Gorduin Wynlar's presence because he maintains the calibration records that make the year-over-year comparison possible. When he goes on research travel, the ritual is postponed.
