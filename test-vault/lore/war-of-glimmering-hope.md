# War of Glimmering Hope

The War of Glimmering Hope is not a war in the traditional sense — not yet. It is the name already being applied by historians to the conflict that most believe is coming, as the discovery of the functioning resonance grid fragment beneath [[Rappiop Grove]] has set the three Triumvirate houses on a collision course that conventional diplomacy has so far failed to resolve.

The name was coined by a satirical broadsheet that noted the irony: a civilization destroyed itself once pursuing the same technology. The hope glimmers because everyone believes they will be the one to use it responsibly. The war glimmers because they are all wrong.

Current status: no shots fired. Three private armies quietly mobilized. The [[Oracles of Might]] have warded the research site. The [[Wardenship]] has stationed seven senior practitioners at the perimeter. The [[Solarguard]] is running triple patrol rotations in [[Bellsummit]].

The [[Binding Compact]] established in Year 145 theoretically prohibits armed conflict between Triumvirate houses within city limits. Most legal scholars believe it will not survive contact with the actual conflict.

This entry is referenced by the [[Angel Crown]] through an inscription that changes — current reading: "When the hope glimmers, close your eyes."
