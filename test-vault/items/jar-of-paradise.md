# Jar of Paradise

A sealed glass jar containing a self-sustaining ecosystem approximately the size of a small room when accessed from inside. Origin unknown. Currently in the possession of a [[Highwallow]] herbalist who uses it to grow medicinal plants year-round. She declines all offers to purchase it.
