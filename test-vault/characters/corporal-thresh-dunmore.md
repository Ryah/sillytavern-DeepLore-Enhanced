# Corporal Thresh Dunmore

[[Solarguard]] corporal assigned to gate duty at [[Bellsummit]]'s eastern approach. He has been in this posting for three years and knows every regular face that passes his gate. He has a prodigious memory for faces and a principled objection to being bribed — not on moral grounds, but because he calculated once that the risks outweigh the income. He will, however, accept bribes in the form of good information, which he considers a different category of transaction.
