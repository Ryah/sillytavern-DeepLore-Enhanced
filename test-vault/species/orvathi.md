# Orvathi

Orvathi are nocturnal humanoids with excellent low-light vision and significantly reduced daytime functionality. They dominate the night-shift economy in Duskfrost — night watchmen, late-hours tavern staff, and the informal night market in [[Amberburgh]] are majority-Orvathi.

Specimens have been documented near [[Scorchhelm]].
