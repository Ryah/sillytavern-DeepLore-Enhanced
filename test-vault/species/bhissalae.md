# Bhissalae

The Bhissalae are an amphibious species smaller than [[Zuvine]], with mottled green-brown skin. They are the primary competitors of the Zuvine in the underwater salvage trade. Relations between the two species are formally neutral and practically hostile.

Specimens have been documented near [[Bellsummit]].
