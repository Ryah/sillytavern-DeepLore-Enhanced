AI Notebook

Automatically capture session notes from AI responses. Notes are stored per-chat and reinjected into future messages so the AI remembers what happened.

The writing AI includes <dle-notes> tags in its response. No extra API calls — notes are extracted from the response text. Best with flagship models (Claude Opus/Sonnet, GPT-4o). Smaller models may not follow the format.
After each response, DLE sends the text to your AI Search connection to extract session notes. Works with any writing model — the extraction uses a separate, reliable call. Visible "Note to self:" prose is automatically stripped. Costs one extra API call per message.
