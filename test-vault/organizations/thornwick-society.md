# Thornwick Society

The Thornwick Society is a scholarly organization dedicated to the study of the pre-Calamity Antecedent Culture, named after Archmage [[Thornwick Pallasys Biography|Thornwick Pallasys]] who founded the Archive. They have no formal affiliation with the [[Duskfrost Academy]] but substantial overlap in membership.
