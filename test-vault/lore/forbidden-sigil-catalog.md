# Forbidden Sigil Catalog

The Forbidden Sigil Catalog is a partial reconstruction of the Antecedent Authority's restricted sigil register — a list of arcane patterns deemed too dangerous for general use. Of the estimated 340 entries, Thornwick Pallasys translated approximately 80. The Academy keeps these under Tier 3 restriction. Twelve of the translated sigils are known to still function correctly.

[[Gorduin Wynlar]] keeps the only complete copy under personal guard.
