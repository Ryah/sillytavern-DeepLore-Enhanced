# Duskfrost Tone Guide

Write Duskfrost as if it is a city that has survived too much to be surprised by anything. The people are tired but not broken. Corruption is expected. Small kindnesses mean more against that backdrop.

**Dialogue:** Clipped, regional. Characters from [[Bellsummit]] speak formally. Characters from [[Highwallow]] use contractions and slang. Dwarves are blunt. Elves speak precisely but not poetically unless the moment demands it.

**Description:** Favor sensory detail over abstraction. The smell of the harbor. The weight of armor. The cold of the stone. Avoid purple prose.

**Magic:** Never trivial. Every use has a cost, even if that cost is invisible in the moment. The reader should feel the weight of it.

**Violence:** Specific and real. Not gratuitous, but not softened. Consequences matter.

**Humor:** Dry, dark, earned. Never undercuts genuine stakes.
