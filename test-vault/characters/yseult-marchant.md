# Yseult Marchant

Human luxury goods trader based in [[Amberburgh]]'s upper commercial district, specializing in imported textiles, rare pigments, and the kind of goods that require connections rather than just capital to source. She cultivates relationships with foreign trading houses that most Amberburgh merchants cannot access, which makes her goods genuinely rare and her social calendar full. She is a reliable source of information about what wealthy people are currently buying, which is often more interesting than it sounds.

Yseult grew up in Highwallow — a fact she has not lied about but has not volunteered in fifteen years. Her accent, her wardrobe, and her manners were all acquired through deliberate study of the merchant class she intended to join, and she joined it by being smarter about foreign sourcing than merchants who had been trading for decades. Her first profitable shipment was Stormhaven indigo dye, acquired through a contact nobody else in Amberburgh could reach because nobody else had bothered to learn the Stormhaven trade dialect. She considers language her primary business tool.

What her clients do not know is that Yseult tracks purchasing patterns the way an intelligence analyst tracks communications — who is buying preservation-grade materials (scholars and archivists preparing for something), who is buying unusual quantities of pigment (artists or forgers), who has suddenly stopped buying luxury goods (financial trouble or departure). She does not sell this analysis. She uses it to position her inventory and to maintain the one advantage that keeps her competitive: knowing what people will want before they know it themselves.

She provides [[Master Aldric Voss]] with intelligence from her foreign trading contacts in exchange for favorable treatment from the Consortium, an arrangement that benefits both and that she considers the most honest transaction in Amberburgh — two people exchanging value without pretending it is friendship.
