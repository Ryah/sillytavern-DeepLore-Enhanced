# Velhari

Velhari are humanoids with a natural ability to generate minor light-based illusions — flickering shapes, color shifts, simple visual noise. Most use this ability for performance or trade. A minority use it for less legal purposes.

A small but established community resides in the lower districts of [[Bellsummit]].
