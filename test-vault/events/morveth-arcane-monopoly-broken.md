# Breaking of the Morveth Arcane Monopoly

Year 147. The founding of the Academy was primarily a political act: the other two Triumvirate houses had tolerated House Morveth's monopoly on surviving arcane knowledge for a century, and they were done tolerating it. The Academy's founding charter transferred control of the Morveth private archive to a jointly governed institution. House Morveth received significant agricultural concessions in exchange.

The resulting institution became the [[Duskfrost Academy]], which has outlasted the political circumstances of its creation.
