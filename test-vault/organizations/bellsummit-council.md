# Bellsummit District Council

The Bellsummit District Council manages local administration for the high district — street maintenance, licensing, minor disputes. Technically subordinate to the Triumvirate; in practice, the Council does what it wants because its members are all Triumvirate-adjacent nobility.

Three current council members are active in the [[Academy Alumni Fellowship]], which provides a useful informal channel to the Academy administration.
