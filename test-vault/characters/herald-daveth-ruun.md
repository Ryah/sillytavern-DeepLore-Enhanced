# Herald Daveth Ruun

Personal aide and herald to the First Speaker of the Triumvirate — the person who controls access to the highest level of Triumvirate executive authority. He is the last gatekeeper before the First Speaker's schedule, the person who decides which communications are urgent enough to interrupt and which can wait. He has held this position for eight years across two First Speakers, which means his institutional knowledge substantially exceeds that of his current principal.

His position within the [[Duskfrost Triumvirate]] makes him one of the most politically sensitive appointments in the city.
