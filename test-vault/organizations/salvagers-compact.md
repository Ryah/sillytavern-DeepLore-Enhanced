# Salvagers Compact

The Salvagers Compact holds the city license for underwater salvage work in the harbor. Membership is majority [[Zuvine]] and [[Bhissalae]], with significant internal tension between the two species — the kind of tension that comes from people who are very good at the same job and disagree fundamentally about how it should be done. The [[Duskfrost Academy]] buys everything the Compact brings up that it deems arcane, which is most of the valuable finds and the primary reason the Compact exists as a going concern.

The harbor floor is a graveyard of pre-[[The Calamity Onset|Calamity]] construction, sunken cargo, and three centuries of objects that Duskfrost has dropped, dumped, or deliberately hidden in the water. The Compact's divers work the harbor in rotating crews — Zuvine divers handling the deeper, longer-duration work thanks to their aquatic physiology, Bhissalae divers specializing in the shallower structural assessment and delicate extraction that their finer manipulators are suited for. This division of labor is practical and resentful. The Zuvine majority, led by Compact-Master Dreva Saltborn, controls the dive schedules and claims priority on high-value recovery sites. The Bhissalae minority argues, with some justification, that their assessment work identifies the valuable sites in the first place and they should share proportionally in the revenue.

The Academy relationship is the Compact's economic backbone and its ethical headache. Academy purchasing agents evaluate every haul that might have arcane significance — resonance-touched artifacts, pre-Calamity mechanisms, anything that hums, glows, or makes instruments behave strangely — and pay above market rate for it. The Compact is contractually obligated to report all such finds. In practice, the most interesting pieces occasionally detour through the [[Bonehands]]' less documented channels before reaching the Academy, a fact that Dreva Saltborn officially knows nothing about and practically manages with careful inattention. Several Compact members hold dual membership in the [[Academy Alumni Fellowship]], which provides useful advance notice of what the Academy is currently looking for.

The Compact's deeper tension is territorial. As harbor trade expands and the [[Velkhast Trading Company]] pushes for new berth construction, salvage zones are being built over. The Compact has petitioned the Triumvirate for protected salvage areas three times. Three times the petition has been weighed against harbor commerce revenue and found wanting. Dreva Saltborn watches the construction pilings go into the harbor floor and calculates how many pre-Calamity sites are being permanently buried beneath them. She is not a sentimental person, but the harbor remembers things that Duskfrost has chosen to forget, and someone should be bringing them up before they're sealed under concrete forever.
