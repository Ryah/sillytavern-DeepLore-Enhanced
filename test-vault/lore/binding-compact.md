# Binding Compact

The Binding Compact (Year 145) is the foundational legal agreement that prohibits open armed conflict between Triumvirate houses within Duskfrost city limits. It was signed in [[Bellsummit]] following the Second Consolidation War, which damaged three districts and killed approximately two thousand people.

The Compact is enforced by a joint tribunal — two representatives from each house, two from the Academy — with authority to levy severe economic penalties and, in extreme cases, revoke a house's governance rights for a period of up to ten years.

The Compact has held, with one major exception (the [[War of the Broken Mountain]] in Year 289, which the tribunal retroactively classified as "enforcement action" to avoid triggering revocation proceedings), for three centuries.

Its current relevance is acute. The [[War of Glimmering Hope]] scenario — if it becomes actual conflict — will test whether the Compact's language covers the situation. The key disputed phrase is "armed conflict," which some legal scholars argue requires a formal declaration to trigger. Others disagree vigorously.

The physical Compact document is stored in the [[Scorchhelm]] fortress archive, in a vault that requires three keys — one held by each Triumvirate house. The Academy is the only party with access to all three without asking anyone's permission, a fact that has been noted but never publicly addressed.

The [[Oracles of Might]] published a legal analysis of the Compact's applicability to the current situation. The analysis concluded, diplomatically, that the answer "depends heavily on specific circumstances." All three houses hired their own legal scholars to interpret the analysis. None reached the same conclusion.
