# Rappiop Grove

A park and garden neighborhood in [[Amberburgh]] maintained under Academy contract. Known for its living hedges ([[Thyren]]-grown), outdoor study pavilions, and the fact that nearly everyone who meets clandestinely in Duskfrost eventually ends up in Rappiop Grove. The resonance grid fragment was discovered beneath the eastern garden.
