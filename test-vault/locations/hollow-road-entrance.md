# Hollow Road Entrance — Highwallow

A partially collapsed building in Highwallow conceals the most accessible known entrance to [[The Hollow Roads]] network. The Academy has mapped the first kilometer. The [[Gnaino]] community knows precisely how far the network extends and has not shared this information with anyone.
