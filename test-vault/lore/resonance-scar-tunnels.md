# Resonance Scar Tunnels

[[The Hollow Roads]] pass beneath the Resonance Scar, and the tunnels in that section are unlike the rest of the network. The walls emit low-level light that varies with the Scar's activity cycle. Navigation instruments fail. Three Academy survey teams have entered; two returned with complete data, one returned with incomplete data and no explanation for the missing six hours. Access is currently restricted.

Understanding requires familiarity with the [[Mage Dueling Code]].
